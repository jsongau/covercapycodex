# 03 — Content outline: contact.html

Voice: concierge desk. Warm, precise, no overpromising on response time (we have no published SLA,
so state expectations softly without inventing hours). No em-dashes, no roman numerals.

## Hero
- Eyebrow: "CoverCapy / Concierge"
- H1: "Contact CoverCapy"
- Subhead: one place to reach the desk, for patients and for dentists.
- Primary email button: contact@covercapy.com (mailto).

## Section — Two lanes
Two cards side by side:
- For patients: PPO plan options, treatment cost estimates, dentist discovery. Links to compare,
  find-my-dentist, estimator, patient portal (/join/).
- For dentists and practices: claim or update a profile, Capy Accreditation, sponsored listings,
  listing corrections. Links to dentist-portal, capy-practice-membership, capy-accreditation.

## Section — Response expectations
Soft statement: the concierge reads every message and routes it to the right desk; include the
relevant page URL so we can help faster. No invented SLA.

## Section — Privacy, accessibility, legal
How to send those requests (include page URL + clear description). Links to privacy/accessibility/terms.

## Section — Emergency notice
Verbatim intent from contacts.html: CoverCapy does not provide emergency care; contact a licensed
professional, urgent care, or emergency services.
