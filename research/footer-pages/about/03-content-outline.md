# 03 — Content outline: about.html

Voice: editorial luxury, boutique-hotel concierge. Calm, confident, no hype, no em-dashes,
no roman numerals. First-person plural ("we") for mission; never invent founders or metrics.

## Section order

1. HERO
   - Eyebrow: "CoverCapy / About"
   - H1: "We turn the messiest moment in dental care into the calmest."
   - Lead: CoverCapy is a concierge for PPO dental care. The tagline lives here:
     "Get cover today, see a dentist tomorrow."
   - Primary CTA: Compare PPO plans. Secondary: Find an in-network dentist.
   - Quick-answer TL;DR block (GEO): one or two sentences defining the company + "not an insurer."

2. THE PROBLEM — "People do not shop for insurance. They shop for a moment."
   - The insight: a cracked tooth, a new baby, a wedding, a move, a job change, a kid's first
     cleaning. People search for care around life events, not around carrier brand names.
   - At that moment they face three unknowns: am I covered, what will it cost, who near me
     actually takes my plan. Every office online says the same words: gentle, modern, caring.
   - Light external context: patient acquisition is expensive and patients churn when the
     experience is confusing (cite Patientdesk.ai). The friction is the product gap.

3. THE APPROACH — "A concierge, not a portal."
   - We are not an insurance-comparison grid and not a healthcare directory. We sit beside the
     patient like a good concierge: clarify coverage, estimate cost, confirm the office takes
     the exact PPO network, and hand off cleanly to a real dentist.
   - Three pillars: Coverage clarity / Cost honesty / Verified discovery.
   - Reference eligibility verification as "Plaid for dental eligibility" framed plainly:
     a quick check that confirms a plan is accepted before the visit. Member ID never stored.

4. HOW WE HELP PATIENTS
   - Compare PPO plans without the jargon. Estimate what you will actually owe.
   - Verify coverage at a specific office. Find a modern, in-network dentist near you.
   - Free for patients. Restate: not an insurer, not a practice. Link out to compare + find.

5. HOW WE HELP DENTISTS
   - Modern practices struggle to reach coverage-aware patients ready to book.
   - We give them a clean, verified profile and connect them with the right patients.
   - Claim a free profile, earn Capy Accreditation, or upgrade visibility. Placement is
     reviewed and labeled, never sold as guaranteed volume. Link to dentist portal + membership.

6. THE STANDARD — Capy Accreditation
   - Why a standard exists: claims are easy to write, hard to verify. Accreditation replaces
     vague promises with reviewed signals of modern, patient-first care.
   - Selective by design: membership begins a review; not every applicant is approved.
   - Link to /capy-accreditation.html and /capy-accredited-dentists.html.

7. TRUST + TRANSPARENCY
   - Plain statements: we are not an insurance carrier or a dental practice. Sponsored or
     featured listings are labeled. Patient tools are free; revenue comes from practice
     programs. Member IDs are never stored. Always verify benefits and credentials directly.
   - This section mirrors footer fineprint truthfully.

8. THE NETWORK (bridge)
   - Short editorial bridge: every verified office and every prepared patient makes the next
     match better. Link to /covercapy-network-effect.html ("how the CoverCapy network compounds").

9. FINAL CTA
   - "Enter dental care prepared." Buttons: Compare PPO plans / Find a dentist / Dentist portal.

10. Page legal note (echo footer disclaimer truthfully, scoped).
