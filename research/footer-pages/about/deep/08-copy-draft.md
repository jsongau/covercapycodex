## SECTION 2 — THE PROBLEM (life-event framing)

[EYEBROW] Why We Exist

[H2]
People do not shop for insurance. They shop for a moment.

[BODY]
A cracked tooth on a Sunday night. A new baby and a first set of benefits no one has read
yet. A wedding photo six weeks away. A move to a city where you know no one. A job change that
quietly swapped one dental plan for another. These are the moments that send people looking for
a dentist. Almost no one wakes up wanting to study a carrier brand.

At that moment, three questions arrive at once: are you actually covered, what will this really
cost you, and who near you takes your exact plan. The honest answer is usually a shrug. There is
no shortage of dentists and no shortage of insurance. The shortage is an answer at the exact
moment a person needs one. Every practice online describes itself with the same words, gentle,
modern, advanced, caring, and those words are easy to write and hard for a patient to confirm
from a website.

Offices feel the same gap from the other side. Reaching the right patient is expensive, and
people walk away when the experience is confusing rather than clear. So the friction is not a
lack of dentists and not a lack of insurance. It is the distance between the two, at the precise
moment a person needs to decide. CoverCapy exists to close that distance with a calm, prepared
handoff.

[SIDE PANEL — "What CoverCapy is" / "Is not"]
- Is: a concierge that clarifies coverage, cost, and in-network fit before you go.
- Is: a discovery layer for modern, in-network PPO dentists across the United States.
- Is not: an insurance carrier, broker of record, or a dental practice.
- Is not: a generic comparison grid or a plain healthcare directory.

## SECTION 3 — WHAT COVERCAPY IS / WHAT WE DO

[EYEBROW] Our Approach

[H2]
A concierge, not a portal.

[INTRO LEAD]
We do not hand you a spreadsheet and wish you luck. We sit beside you the way a good concierge
would, then step back and let a real dentist take care of you.

[BODY]
Think of CoverCapy as the layer that prepares you before the chair. We organize dental care
around the moments people are actually living through, not around the carrier logos the industry
is built on. Everything we do reduces to four quiet jobs: make coverage clear, make cost honest,
confirm the office in-network, and hand you off ready. Each of the four is a real service, not a
slogan.

[PILLAR 1 — Coverage clarity]
We translate PPO plans into plain language, so you know what your plan does and does not cover
before you sit in the chair, not after the bill arrives. No jargon, no fine print you have to
decode alone.

[PILLAR 2 — Cost honesty]
We help you estimate what you will actually owe, in ranges rather than false precision, so the
bill is never the surprise. The numbers we show are illustrative, meant to prepare you, never a
quote and never a promise of a final price.

[PILLAR 3 — Verified discovery]
We confirm a dentist takes your exact PPO network, and we surface modern, in-network offices
near you. Think of it as a coverage check for dental, run before the visit. Your member ID is
never stored. We record only that verification was provided, nothing more.

[PILLAR 4 — A clean handoff]
When the clarity is done, we get out of the way. You arrive at a real, modern office already
knowing your network and your likely cost, which is a better start for you and a better patient
for the practice.

## SECTION 5 — OUR STANDARD (Capy Accreditation)

[EYEBROW] The Standard

[H2]
Why we built Capy Accreditation.

[BODY]
Claims are easy. Verification is the hard part, and it is the part a patient cannot do alone from
a website. So we built a standard. Capy Accreditation is a private CoverCapy credential that
reviews practices against published markers of modern, technology-forward, patient-first care. It
replaces vague promises with reviewed signals.

It is selective by design. Membership begins a review, it does not grant the credential. An
office can be approved, conditionally approved, asked to improve first, or declined. If every
office automatically qualified, the credential would mean nothing, and that is exactly the point.
It is not a government certification and not a dental license, and it never replaces your own
check of a provider's credentials.

[CALLOUT]
When you see a Capy Accredited office, you are seeing a practice CoverCapy has actually reviewed,
not just one that wrote nice words about itself.

- Browse Capy Accredited dentists  →  /capy-accredited-dentists.html

## SECTION 6 — TRUST AND TRANSPARENCY

[EYEBROW] Trust And Transparency

[H2]
Where we stand, plainly.

[TRUST ITEM 1 — We are not an insurer]
CoverCapy does not sell, underwrite, or administer insurance, and we are not a dental practice.
We are an independent guidance and discovery platform.

[TRUST ITEM 2 — Patient tools are free]
Comparing plans, estimating costs, verifying coverage, and finding dentists are free for
patients. Our revenue comes from practice programs, not from you.

[TRUST ITEM 3 — How we make money]
We keep patient tools free and earn revenue from dental practices through membership,
accreditation, and visibility programs. When a placement is sponsored or featured, it is clearly
labeled as such, and guidance is never sold to the highest bidder.

[TRUST ITEM 4 — Your member ID stays yours]
When we verify coverage, we record only that verification was provided. The member ID itself is
never stored on our platform.

[TRUST ITEM 5 — Always verify directly]
We help you prepare, but benefits, pricing, network participation, and credentials should always
be confirmed directly with your carrier and the office before you make a care decision.

[TRUST ITEM 6 — Built by insiders]
CoverCapy is built with a working knowledge of how dental offices and PPO networks actually
operate, from eligibility checks to estimates to the money conversation at the front desk, so the
guidance reflects the real chair-side experience rather than a marketing version of it.
