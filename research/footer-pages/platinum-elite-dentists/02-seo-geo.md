## FAQ (these go verbatim into FAQPage schema; 40-60 words each)
1. **What is a Platinum Elite dentist?** A Platinum Elite dentist holds the highest membership tier in
   CoverCapy's directory. It is the top level of Capy Accreditation, limited at the county level, and reserved
   for practices with advanced technology and a proven patient experience. It carries premium placement and the
   widest search reach in the platform.
2. **How much does Platinum Elite cost?** Platinum Elite is $1,499 per month. It is the highest CoverCapy tier,
   above the free listing and Capy Accredited membership. The price reflects county-limited exclusivity, top
   directory placement, an 18-mile search reach, and eligibility for structured CoverCapy programs. It is granted
   by review, not by payment alone.
3. **Do I have to be Capy Accredited first?** Yes. Platinum Elite is the highest level of Capy Accreditation, so a
   practice must already be Capy Accredited before it can progress to Platinum Elite. The review then assesses
   technology, patient experience, and the infrastructure to deliver premium CoverCapy programs well.
4. **What does county-level exclusivity mean?** Platinum Elite is limited by geography and local market density,
   so only a small number of practices can hold it in a given county. When a market is full, new applicants join a
   review queue. Exclusivity is what keeps the tier meaningful, so it is intentionally scarce.
5. **What is the search radius for Platinum Elite?** Platinum Elite carries an 18-mile search reach, the widest in
   CoverCapy. Free listings reach 8 miles and Capy Accredited reaches 10 miles. A wider radius means a Platinum
   office can appear to patients across a broader area when they search for a PPO dentist.
6. **Does Platinum Elite guarantee more patients or a top ranking position?** No. Platinum Elite carries premium,
   clearly labeled placement and the widest reach, but CoverCapy does not promise a specific ranking position, a
   number of patients, or new revenue. Where an office appears is influenced by status, profile completeness, local
   availability, patient trust indicators, and participation.
7. **How do I qualify and apply for Platinum Elite?** First become Capy Accredited through CoverCapy. From there,
   ask about Platinum Elite. The review assesses technology such as required iTero scanning, advanced diagnostics,
   cosmetic and implant capability, patient trust, and program readiness, and confirms whether a place is open in
   your county.
8. **Is Platinum Elite a dental license or certification?** No. Platinum Elite is a private CoverCapy credential and
   directory tier. It is not a government certification or a dental board license and does not replace either. It
   does not guarantee a clinical outcome. It signals a reviewed, technology forward, premium practice inside CoverCapy.
