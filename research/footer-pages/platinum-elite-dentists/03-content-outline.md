## Honesty rules baked into copy
- Always say "by review" / "not by payment alone".
- Never promise a ranking position, patient count, or revenue.
- "county-limited" framed as scarcity, with a queue when full.
- Prices EXACTLY: $0, ~$285/mo (founder), $1,499/mo. Radii: 8 / 10 / 18 miles.
