## Search intent
Transactional / action. The visitor either has a dentist they want confirmed or added, or
wants the concierge to find one nearby. They convert by submitting the form. Lower informational
weight, higher action weight, so the page is short, warm, and form-led.

## Title (under ~60 chars)
Request a Dentist: Concierge PPO Dentist Help | CoverCapy

## Meta description (150-160)
Ask CoverCapy to add or verify your dentist, or find an in-network PPO dentist in your area. Tell us your city and carrier and our concierge handles the rest. Free.

## Canonical
https://www.covercapy.com/request-a-dentist

## Extractable quick answer (GEO / AI Overviews)
You can ask CoverCapy to add or verify a specific dentist, or to find an in-network PPO dentist
in your area, by submitting the Request a Dentist form with your name, email, city or ZIP, and
optionally the practice name and your PPO carrier. CoverCapy's concierge replies with options
and coverage details. CoverCapy never asks for your member ID on this form.

## FAQ Q&A (verbatim into FAQPage schema; 40-60 words each, standalone answers)
1. How do I request a dentist on CoverCapy? — Fill out the Request a Dentist form with your
   name, email, and city or ZIP. Add the practice name if you have one, and your PPO carrier if
   you know it. CoverCapy's concierge then verifies coverage or finds in-network options and
   replies to you, free of charge.
2. Can CoverCapy add my current dentist? — Yes. Enter your dentist's practice name and city and
   CoverCapy will check whether the office is in network or can join CoverCapy, then confirm
   what your PPO plan covers there. There is no cost to ask.
3. Do I have to give my insurance member ID? — No. The Request a Dentist form never asks for
   your member ID, group number, or Social Security number. You only share your name, contact
   details, location, and optionally a carrier name, so CoverCapy can route your request.
4. Is requesting a dentist free? — Yes. Submitting a request and getting concierge help to find
   or verify a PPO dentist is free. CoverCapy makes money through optional membership plans and
   accredited practices, not by charging patients to ask for help.
5. How fast will CoverCapy respond? — CoverCapy aims to reply by email within one business day.
   If you add a phone number, the concierge may call with options. Response time can vary with
   carrier confirmations and office availability in your area.
6. What if I do not have insurance yet? — Choose "I do not have insurance yet" as your carrier
   and CoverCapy can show PPO dental plans that start fast, then help you find an in-network
   dentist. You can compare plans first on the Compare PPO Dental Plans page.
