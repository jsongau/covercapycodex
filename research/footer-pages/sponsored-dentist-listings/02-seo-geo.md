## Extractable quick answer (GEO / AI Overviews)
A sponsored dentist listing on CoverCapy is a paid, clearly labeled placement that gives a dental
practice greater visibility inside the CoverCapy PPO directory and search. Placement is influenced
by membership tier, profile completeness, and trust signals. It never guarantees a specific
position or a set number of patients, and every sponsored slot is disclosed.
