## FAQ Q&A (FAQPage schema; 40-60 words each)

1. What is a network effect?
A network effect is a demand-side advantage where each new participant makes the service more
valuable for everyone else. In a two-sided marketplace, more quality supply improves the
experience for demand, which attracts more demand, which in turn attracts more supply, creating
a self-reinforcing flywheel.

2. What is the CoverCapy network effect?
It is the compounding advantage that grows as verified PPO dentists and coverage-aware patients
reinforce one another on CoverCapy. Five loops drive it: eligibility verification, the first
dentist after coverage, patients recruiting dentists, gamified participation, and an accreditation
standard. Each match makes the next match better.

3. What is dental eligibility verification?
Dental eligibility verification confirms whether a specific office accepts a patient's exact PPO
network before the visit. CoverCapy treats it like a coverage check, removing the most common
source of dental anxiety. The member ID itself is never stored, only the fact that verification
was provided.

4. What does "first dentist after purchase" mean?
After someone secures dental coverage, the office they are matched to first often becomes their
long-term dentist. Being the verified, in-network, modern office at that moment turns a one-time
coverage event into a lasting relationship, which is why that first match is so valuable.

5. How do patients recruit dentists on CoverCapy?
Patients can nominate the office they already trust. This pulls supply onto the platform through
demand rather than cold sales, so practices arrive invited by the people who value them. It is a
loop where happy patients quietly grow the network.

6. What are Capy Crowns?
Capy Crowns are part of CoverCapy's gamified trust layer. Participation, profile completeness,
accreditation, and patient trust indicators feed recognition and ranking signals. Higher supply-
side engagement improves demand-side quality. CoverCapy never promises a specific ranking
position or a guaranteed number of patients.

7. Why does the CoverCapy network compound over time?
Every verified office and every prepared patient improves the next match. Higher average quality
raises trust, which attracts more patients, which makes the network more valuable to join. Data
from verifications and matches makes recommendations sharper, layering a data advantage on the
marketplace.
