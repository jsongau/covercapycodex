- PPO dental coverage by state

## Search intent
Navigational + early-funnel research. The visitor knows their state but not their city/metro
and wants a clean jump-off to the right hub. Also a strong AI-answer-engine target for
"which states does CoverCapy cover" and "PPO dentist directory by state" prompts.

## Title / description (final)
- Title: `PPO Dentists by State — CoverCapy National Directory | CoverCapy`
- Description: `Browse insurance-verified PPO dentists by state. CoverCapy covers thousands of offices across every market we serve. Find your state, then your city, and verify coverage free.`

## Extractable quick answer (GEO)
"CoverCapy lists insurance-verified PPO dentists across every state it serves. Pick your
state to open its hub, then drill down to your metro and city to find offices that accept
your PPO carrier and verify your coverage for free."

## FAQ Q&A (not required for an index page, but recommended if a small FAQ block is added)
1. Q: How do I find a PPO dentist in my state? A: Open the state from this directory, then
   choose your metro and city. Each office page shows the PPO carriers it accepts so you can
   confirm yours before booking.
2. Q: Which states does CoverCapy cover? A: The directory lists every state with at least one
   listed market. The set grows as new markets are added; this page updates automatically on
   each build.

## Internal-link value
This is a top-of-tree hub node. It collapses crawl distance to all 15 state hubs (and
transitively the ~983 market/city URLs and 6,400+ profiles) into a single page, and it is
itself linked sitewide from the footer. That makes it one of the highest-PageRank internal
distributors on the site: link equity entering the footer flows through `/states.html` down
into every geo hub.
