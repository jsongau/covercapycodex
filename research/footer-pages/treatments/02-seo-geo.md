# 02-seo-geo.md — `/treatments.html`

## Primary keyword
- dental treatment costs (PPO)

## Secondary / long-tail cluster
- dental procedure cost guide
- how much does a {implant / crown / root canal / Invisalign / denture} cost
- what does PPO dental insurance cover
- dental treatment cost with insurance
- cosmetic vs covered dental work
- emergency dental cost

## Search intent
Research / cost-comparison. The visitor wants a price range for a specific procedure and to
understand what their PPO covers before booking. This page is the hub that routes them to the
right cost guide. Strong AI-answer-engine target for "how much does {treatment} cost" and
"what dental work does PPO cover" prompts.

## Title / description (final)
- Title: `Dental Treatments and Costs — PPO Coverage Guides | CoverCapy`
- Description: `Treatment-by-treatment cost guides for PPO dental patients. See typical price ranges for implants, crowns, Invisalign, cosmetic, emergency and more, and how PPO coverage and CoverCapy lower the bill.`

## Extractable quick answer (GEO)
"CoverCapy publishes cost guides for the dental treatments people search for most, including
implants, crowns, Invisalign, cosmetic work and emergency care. Each guide shows typical price
ranges and how a PPO plan and CoverCapy reduce what you actually pay."

## FAQ Q&A (optional small block; the cost detail lives on each treatment page)
1. Q: How much do common dental treatments cost? A: It varies by procedure, material and
   region. Open the matching cost guide for a sourced range, and see how much a PPO typically
   covers so you can estimate your out-of-pocket share.
2. Q: Does PPO dental insurance cover these treatments? A: Preventive care is usually covered
   in full, basic and major work at a percentage, and cosmetic work often not at all. Each
   treatment guide explains the coverage tier for that procedure.

## Internal-link value
This is the topical-cluster head for all cost/procedure content. It consolidates the cost
guides (which otherwise only interlink pairwise) under one parent, so search engines read them
as a single authoritative "dental treatment cost" cluster, and footer link equity flows
through this hub into every guide. As the procedure-page library grows, the existence-gated
manifest keeps the hub complete with no maintenance.
