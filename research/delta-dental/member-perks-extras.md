# Delta Dental Member Perks and Value-Add Programs

**One-line summary:** Delta Dental bundles a set of member extras (SmileWay Wellness Benefits, LASIK via QualSight, hearing aids via Amplifon, LifePerks lifestyle discounts, virtual dentistry, and a Healthy Living Hub) where most are access-to-savings or third-party discount programs, NOT insured benefits, and availability varies by plan, employer, and state.

---

## The critical caveat (read first)

Delta Dental draws a hard line between two things:

1. **Insured benefits** (covered under your dental plan, subject to maximums, deductibles, and coinsurance).
2. **Member perks / discount programs** (negotiated access to savings through outside partners like QualSight, Amplifon, and LifeCare's LifePerks).

Per Delta Dental's own FAQ: "All Delta Dental members have access to the wellness library and discounts through LifePerks, QualSight and Amplifon. Wellness benefits such as virtual dentistry are available to all members but may not be covered by your plan." LASIK, hearing aids, and LifePerks lifestyle deals are **savings programs, not insurance**. They give you a discounted price from a partner, not a claim paid by Delta Dental. SmileWay Wellness Benefits and (in many regions) virtual dentistry are the exceptions that ARE actual covered benefits, but only on plans that include them.

Availability of every item below varies by plan, by employer election, and by state / underwriting company.

[Source: Delta Dental Member perks](https://www1.deltadentalins.com/memberperks.html)

## 1. SmileWay Wellness Benefits (an insured benefit, not a discount)

This is the one perk that is genuinely **enhanced dental coverage**, not a discount card. It gives members with certain chronic medical conditions extra cleanings paid at 100%.

**Who qualifies:** You or a covered dependent must have been diagnosed with one of these chronic medical conditions:

- Amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS)
- Cancer
- Chronic kidney disease
- Diabetes
- Heart disease
- HIV/AIDS
- Huntington's disease
- Joint replacement
- Lupus
- Opioid misuse and addiction
- Parkinson's disease
- Rheumatoid arthritis
- Sjögren's syndrome
- Stroke

Note on pregnancy: Delta Dental's current national SmileWay page does NOT list pregnancy in its qualifying-condition set (the list above is the live roster). Some older broker bulletins and certain plan variants have referenced pregnancy / perinatal cleanings, so it can appear on specific employer plans, but it is not on the standard national list as of this research.

**What is covered (the oral-systemic / gum-disease rationale):** The program is built around the link between gum disease and overall health for higher-risk members. Covered at 100% per calendar or contract year:

- One periodontal scaling and root planing (deep cleaning to treat gum disease), per quadrant. Codes D4341 (4+ teeth) / D4342 (1 to 3 teeth).
- Plus up to four additional procedures (any combination) from: prophylaxis / standard teeth cleaning (D1110 adult, D1120 child); periodontal maintenance (D4910); scaling in presence of moderate or severe gingival inflammation (D4346).

This is what people mean by "up to four cleanings a year at 100%": the program allows up to four total cleanings/maintenance procedures per year covered fully, on top of the deep-cleaning benefit.

**Important limits / caveats:**

- "SmileWay Wellness Benefits are not included under all plans." Many, but not all, Delta Dental PPO employer-sponsored plans offer it. Known as **SmileWay Enhanced Benefits in Texas**.
- Benefits are still subject to any applicable plan maximums and deductibles per your Evidence of Coverage.
- **You must opt in.** Log in, select SmileWay Wellness Benefits from the menu, and submit a form including the treating physician's name and phone number. Enhanced benefits begin about 24 hours after enrollment. The menu link only appears if your employer elected the coverage. Adult dependents must opt in themselves or grant HIPAA authorization.

[Source: SmileWay Wellness Benefits](https://www1.deltadentalins.com/members/smileway-wellness-benefits.html)

## 2. LASIK vision surgery via QualSight (a savings program, not insurance)

A negotiated-price discount program through QualSight LASIK. Delta Dental is not paying for eye surgery; QualSight provides contracted pricing.

**Program details:**

- Up to **35% off the national average price of LASIK** (compared to the reported overall national LASIK cost per Market Scope LLC 2021; savings may vary by provider).
- Over **800 LASIK locations nationwide** (QualSight provider file, January 2023).
- Surgeons in the network have collectively performed more than 6.5 million procedures.
- LASIK is FSA and HSA eligible.
- You receive a **free consultation** to see if you are eligible.

**How to start:** Call 855-248-2020 or visit qualsight.com (Delta Dental landing page: deltadental.qualsight.com). QualSight provides contracted nationwide pricing, then a free consult.

[Source: Delta Dental Member perks](https://www1.deltadentalins.com/memberperks.html) | [QualSight Delta Dental portal](https://deltadental.qualsight.com/)

## 3. Hearing care and hearing aids via Amplifon (a savings program, not insurance)

A discount and service program through Amplifon Hearing Health Care, with negotiated pricing plus follow-up care. Again, a savings program, not a covered insured benefit.

**Program details:**

- **66% average savings off retail hearing aid pricing** (per Amplifon's internal analysis of savings off MSRP in 2021; varies by manufacturer and technology level).
- Nationwide network of **6,000+ hearing care providers**.
- Broad choice and latest technology from leading hearing aid brands.
- A **60-day risk-free trial** with a 100% money-back guarantee if not satisfied, with no return or restocking fees.
- Comprehensive service program: one year of unlimited **follow-up care** from a participating Amplifon provider, a two-year supply of free batteries (80 cells per ear per year) or a charging station, and a three-year warranty for loss, repairs, or damage (exclusions may apply).

**How to start:** Complete the request form at amplifonusa.com/deltadentalins or call 888-779-1429. A patient care advocate explains the program, helps find a provider, and books the appointment.

[Source: Delta Dental Member perks](https://www1.deltadentalins.com/memberperks.html) | [Amplifon Delta Dental page](https://www.amplifonusa.com/deltadentalins)

## 4. Teledentistry / virtual dental visits (often a covered benefit, varies by plan)

Unlike LASIK and hearing, virtual dentistry can be an actual covered benefit, but coverage depends on the plan.

**The two national tools** (both "covered with a Delta Dental PPO or Delta Dental Premier plan," per the member perks page):

- **Video visits:** a live video consultation with a dentist. Ideal for urgent needs or appointments outside normal office hours.
- **Photo submissions:** a photo-based dental assessment. Ideal for a quick exam and a full diagnostic report including treatment options and costs.

**Regional implementation (TeleDentistry.com):** Several Delta Dental member companies (Arizona, New Mexico, Illinois, Arkansas, and others) run virtual visits through TeleDentistry.com, offering 24/7 access 365 days a year. Typical structure:

- Members with **100% coverage for oral evaluations** who have not exceeded their annual maximum can receive a virtual visit as a covered benefit; deductibles and coinsurance apply. Members with less than 100% coverage pay a calculated coinsurance at time of service.
- The dentist provides an initial consultation and can write prescriptions when appropriate, then refers to a Delta Dental network dentist for definitive diagnosis and treatment.
- Positioned to **supplement** the regular dental plan, for use after hours, on holidays/weekends, or in an emergency when the regular dentist is unavailable.

**Caveat:** "Wellness benefits such as virtual dentistry are available to all members but may not be covered by your plan." Access is broad; coverage (free vs. coinsurance) is plan-specific.

[Source: Delta Dental Member perks](https://www1.deltadentalins.com/memberperks.html) | [Virtual Dentistry](https://www1.deltadentalins.com/members/virtual-dentistry.html) | [Delta Dental of Arizona Teledentistry](https://www.deltadentalaz.com/member/teledentistry/) | [Delta Dental of Arkansas Virtual Visits](https://www.deltadentalar.com/members/virtual-visits)

## 5. LifePerks lifestyle discount program (a discount program, not insurance)

A free-to-members lifestyle discount marketplace run through LifeCare. Pure discount program with no claims and no Delta Dental payment.

**What it covers:** "Unlimited access to discounts on fitness, groceries and meal delivery, as well as travel, entertainment, pet insurance, childcare and more."

Category breakdown reported by Delta Dental:

- **Health & wellness:** nutrition, fitness equipment, gym memberships.
- **Lifestyle:** childcare, groceries, home goods, computers, wireless services, pet care, financial and auto services.
- **Travel & entertainment:** movie theaters, theme parks, vacation planning, travel services.
- **Oral health:** discounts on oral-care items.

Example offers cited by Delta Dental: a Sam's Club one-year membership for $19.99 and up to 20% off Spot Pet Insurance.

**Cost / access:** Zero cost to members or employers, with 24/7 email customer support and no administrative work for employers. Members register via the LifePerks portal.

[Source: Delta Dental Member perks](https://www1.deltadentalins.com/memberperks.html) | [LifePerks lifestyle benefits](https://www1.deltadentalins.com/brokers/insider-update/2023/lifeperks-lifestyle-benefits.html) | [LifePerks registration](https://discountmember.lifecare.com/registration/register1.rtml?service=registration&member=1&corp_or_ccode=DDCACOMM&partner_token=DDCACOM-041023)

## 6. Other extras worth naming

**Healthy Living Hub / wellness library and the oral-systemic angle.** A free content library on oral health and its connection to systemic conditions (diabetes and oral health, gum disease, etc.). Available to all members. This is the editorial backbone of Delta Dental's "your mouth is connected to your body" gum-disease / oral-systemic messaging, and the on-ramp to SmileWay. Not a benefit, just resources.
[Source: Healthy Living Hub](https://www1.deltadentalins.com/wellness.html)

**Pre-treatment estimates as a feature.** Delta Dental promotes pre-treatment estimates (predeterminations) and cost-transparency tools as a member feature so patients can see expected coverage and out-of-pocket cost before treatment. This is a service feature of the plan, not a separate perk.
[Source: Delta Dental Member perks](https://www1.deltadentalins.com/memberperks.html)

**Mobile app / online account.** Member account and Delta Dental mobile app surface the ID card, claims, dentist search, and access points for SmileWay opt-in and virtual visits.

**Lane Health spending card (currently paused).** Delta Dental had partnered with Lane Health on a flexible spending / HSA-style card ("ddsmile"), but the member perks page currently states Lane Health "is currently not accepting new card applications." Flag as on-hold, not a live perk.
[Source: Delta Dental Member perks](https://www1.deltadentalins.com/memberperks.html)

## Implications for the hub

- **Lead with the honest framing as a trust play.** CoverCapy can differentiate by clearly labeling which Delta items are real coverage (SmileWay extra cleanings, often virtual dentistry) versus access-to-savings programs (LASIK, hearing, LifePerks). That "savings program, not insurance" clarity is exactly the concierge-grade honesty the luxury positioning wants, and it preempts the disappointment of members who expect a paid claim and get a coupon.
- **SmileWay is the strongest cross-sell and content hook.** The chronic-condition extra-cleanings benefit (diabetes, heart disease, cancer, etc.) maps directly onto oral-systemic content and onto patients searching for dentists who manage gum disease. It is also a reason to verify coverage, which aligns with CoverCapy's verification wizard. Worth a dedicated explainer page plus a callout on dentist profiles in markets where SmileWay is common.
- **Treat LASIK / Amplifon / LifePerks as "extras you may not know you have."** These are low-effort, high-perceived-value bullets for a "what your Delta plan also gets you" module, but always with the variability disclaimer (varies by plan, employer, and state) and the partner phone numbers so we are accurate and not implying CoverCapy provides them.
- **Flag plan-variance everywhere.** Because availability differs by plan, employer election, and underwriting state, any hub copy should push users to verify in their own account rather than state benefits as guaranteed. This also reinforces the verification CTA pattern.
- **Note the Lane Health pause** so we do not advertise a perk that is not currently accepting applications.
