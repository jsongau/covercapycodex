# Inside Delta Dental: The Largest US Dental Carrier

**One-line summary:** Delta Dental is the largest dental benefits carrier in the United States, a not-for-profit national association of 39 independent member companies that together cover roughly 85 million people in all 50 states, DC, and Puerto Rico, backed by the nation's largest dentist network.

---

## 1. Scale and members

Delta Dental describes itself as protecting "the oral health of more Americans than any other dental benefits company" and as "the nation's leading provider of dental benefits for over 70 years." Its official About page states it provides coverage "in all 50 states, Washington, D.C., Puerto Rico, and other U.S. territories."

- **Total members:** Approximately 85 million people, enrolled in over 157,000 groups. This 85 million figure is the most widely cited, sourced to Delta Dental member-company language and reflected on Wikipedia. Note: Delta's own national About page meta description currently reads "more than 78 million Americans," so the public number has ranged roughly from 78 million to 89 million across different pages and dates. Treat 85 million as the headline figure and 78 to 89 million as the documented range. Any single exact count is best labeled an approximation because each of the 39 companies reports separately.
- **Member companies:** 39 independent Delta Dental companies, coordinated by the not-for-profit Delta Dental Plans Association (DDPA), founded 1966.
- **Community investment:** Over the past 15 years, Delta Dental companies and their foundations invested more than $2.3 billion in oral and overall health programs. In 2023 alone, Delta Dental invested over $106 million in community programs across all 50 states, DC, and Puerto Rico.

## 2. Structure: 39 companies plus the association

"Delta Dental" is not a single insurer. It is a federated network. The Delta Dental Plans Association is a not-for-profit national association; the 39 member companies are independent and operate regionally (for example, Delta Dental of California, Delta Dental of Pennsylvania, Delta Dental of New York, Delta Dental of Washington). Some members are non-profit and a few have for-profit segments. This federated structure is why national totals are aggregated from separate filings rather than one consolidated report.

Largest member group: Delta Dental of California and its affiliates (which include Delta Dental Insurance Company, Delta Dental of Pennsylvania, and Delta Dental of New York) serves more than 45 million members on its own, making it the single largest member group.

## 3. Dentist network

- **Delta Dental PPO network:** More than 112,000 dentists participating in over 278,000 locations nationwide. This figure is published directly on Delta Dental's official "Understanding PPO dental plans" page, sourced there to "Delta Dental Internal Data."
- **Delta Dental Premier network:** Larger than PPO. Premier is Delta's broader open-access network and functions as a safety-net tier; together PPO plus Premier make up what Delta calls the largest network of dentists in the nation, citing Zelis Network360, with roughly 3 of every 4 US dentists participating in one or more Delta networks.
- **Secondary reporting** (label as secondary estimate): some industry write-ups cite Delta at around 128,000 participating dentists across 198,000-plus locations when counting all networks. The 112,000 PPO / 278,000 locations figure is the one to lead with because it is published on Delta's own site.

## 4. Market share and rank

- Delta Dental is consistently described as the **single largest dental insurance provider in the United States** by members covered, with over 80 million people enrolled.
- One industry analysis attributes Delta roughly **11.1% of total US dental insurance industry revenue** (label: third-party estimate, IBISWorld-style market data).
- In its home state, Delta Dental of California holds the highest market share at about **40.3%** of the California dental market (label: state-level estimate).
- Caveat to state honestly: a few sources call MetLife "the single largest insurer of dental care" by provider-network size (146,000-plus providers). By members covered, Delta is number one; by some raw provider-count metrics, MetLife is cited as larger. The honest framing is: Delta leads on members and on PPO-plus-Premier combined network reach; rankings differ depending on whether you measure members, revenue, or provider count.

## 5. Financials

- **Revenue:** Delta is a multi-billion-dollar enterprise in aggregate across its 39 companies. Note: there is no single consolidated public revenue figure. The "$80M annual revenue" figure that appears for "Delta Dental Plans Association" in some business databases refers only to the small national trade association, not the operating companies, and should not be presented as total Delta Dental revenue. Aggregate operating-company premium revenue is in the billions of dollars annually (label: estimate; exact consolidated figure is not publicly reported as one number).

## 6. Ratings

- **AM Best:** Delta Dental of California and its affiliates (Delta Dental Insurance Company, Delta Dental of Pennsylvania, Delta Dental of New York) carry an AM Best Financial Strength Rating of **A (Excellent)** with a Long-Term Issuer Credit Rating of "a" and a stable outlook. AM Best categorizes the group's balance-sheet strength as "very strong."
- **S&P:** A specific S&P Global Ratings grade for Delta Dental of California (sometimes cited informally as "AA-") could not be confirmed from a primary S&P or Delta source in this review. Label as unverified until a primary S&P document is located. Do not publish an S&P letter grade without that confirmation.
- Note: ratings apply to specific operating companies, not to "Delta Dental" as a whole, because each member company is independently capitalized.

## 7. Geographic reach

All 50 states, Washington DC, Puerto Rico, and other US territories. This is confirmed on Delta's official About page. No other dental carrier matches this full federated US-territory footprint with a local member company in every state.

## 8. How Delta compares to the other majors

The major US dental carriers are Delta Dental, MetLife, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare, Aetna, and Guardian. Approximate, cited network/member figures (label all competitor provider counts as secondary-source estimates):

| Carrier | Headline scale | Network size (estimate) |
|---|---|---|
| Delta Dental | ~85M members covered, #1 by members; 39 member companies | PPO 112,000 dentists / 278,000 locations (official); Premier larger; ~3 of 4 US dentists |
| MetLife | Cited by some as largest by provider count | 146,000-plus dentists / providers (secondary estimate) |
| UnitedHealthcare | Major national medical-plus-dental carrier | Large national network (secondary estimate) |
| Cigna | DPPO Advantage Network | 80,000-plus unique providers in 300,000-plus locations (secondary estimate) |
| Guardian | Top-ten dental insurer | 120,000-plus dentists and professionals (secondary estimate) |

Takeaway: Delta leads on members covered and on combined PPO-plus-Premier reach. Competitors lead or tie on isolated metrics (MetLife on certain provider counts, Cigna on raw location count), but no single competitor matches Delta's combination of #1 member base, full 50-state-plus-territory federated footprint, and the largest PPO-plus-Premier dentist network.
