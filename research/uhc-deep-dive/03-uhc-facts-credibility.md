## 1. "Largest insurer" framing — SAFE, EXACT WORDING

### The corporate distinction (use this carefully)
- **UnitedHealth Group (UNH)** is the publicly traded PARENT company. It operates two segments: **UnitedHealthcare** (the health-benefits/insurance arm) and **Optum** (health services / pharmacy benefits).
- **UnitedHealthcare** is the insurance arm of UnitedHealth Group. The UHC Primary Dental plan is a UnitedHealthcare-branded product (see Section 2 for the underwriter).

### ✅ SAFE COPY for the page (defensible exact framing)
> "from UnitedHealthcare, the insurance arm of UnitedHealth Group — the largest US health insurer and, by revenue, the largest health care company in the country ($400+ billion in annual revenue, serving roughly 50 million members)."

Acceptable shorter variants:
- "UnitedHealthcare, the insurance arm of UnitedHealth Group, the largest U.S. health insurer by revenue and membership."
- "Backed by UnitedHealth Group, the largest health care company in the U.S. by revenue (Fortune 500 #3 in 2025)."

**Do NOT say:** "UnitedHealthcare is a Fortune 500 #3 company" (that rank belongs to the PARENT, UnitedHealth Group). Do NOT attribute the $400B revenue or 51M members to "UnitedHealthcare" alone — those are UnitedHealth Group consolidated figures. Always tie the superlative to UnitedHealth Group, then note UnitedHealthcare is its insurance arm.

## 2. Who underwrites UHC Primary Dental — Golden Rule Insurance Company

### Verified facts (primary source: uhone.com)
- UnitedHealthcare-branded individual/family dental plans are **underwritten by Golden Rule Insurance Company, a UnitedHealthcare company.** Source: UnitedHealthOne dental page — https://www.uhone.com/health-insurance/supplemental/dental-insurance (accessed 2026-06-26). Direct quote: "UnitedHealthcare-branded dental insurance plans, underwritten by Golden Rule Insurance Company, are Dental PPOs."
- **Golden Rule history:** "Prior to being purchased by UnitedHealthcare in 2003, Golden Rule Insurance Company had served the insurance needs of families and individuals for decades." Source: UnitedHealthOne About Us — https://www.uhone.com/about-us (accessed 2026-06-26).
- **The UnitedHealthcare family of companies has served individual/family insurance needs since 1946** — "over 80 years of personal insurance experience." Source: same About Us page.
- **UnitedHealthOne** is the BRAND name "used to represent several companies under UnitedHealthcare that provide products and services designed specifically for individuals and families." Some are marketed under the UnitedHealthcare brand. Source: same About Us page.
- Administrative services provided by United HealthCare Services, Inc.; dental/vision products administered by related companies (e.g., Dental Benefit Providers, Inc.). Source: uhone.com footer disclaimers + ehealthinsurance product pages.

### ✅ SAFE underwriter framing
> "UHC Primary Dental is a UnitedHealthcare-branded dental PPO underwritten by Golden Rule Insurance Company, a UnitedHealthcare company. Golden Rule has served individual and family insurance needs since 1946 and joined UnitedHealthcare in 2003 — what you may know as 'UnitedHealthOne.'"

## 3. UnitedHealthcare dental network size

- **The UnitedHealthcare nationwide dental network includes more than 80,000 dentists** (cited across multiple network/provider summaries; UHC describes a "nationwide network of dentists offering services at negotiated lower rates" on uhone.com).
  - Source (figure): HelpAdvisor — https://www.helpadvisor.com/medicare/united-healthcare-dental-providers (accessed 2026-06-26)
  - Source (nationwide network language, primary): UnitedHealthOne dental page — https://www.uhone.com/health-insurance/supplemental/dental-insurance (accessed 2026-06-26)
- ⚠️ Note: secondary sources sometimes cite "more than 80,000 dentists" as a count of UNIQUE dentists vs. access points/locations (a dentist at multiple offices = multiple access points). For safety, state it as **"more than 80,000 dentists nationwide"** and avoid a precise "locations" number unless sourced from UHC marketing directly.

### ✅ SAFE network copy
> "UnitedHealthcare's nationwide dental network includes more than 80,000 dentists offering care at negotiated in-network rates."

## 5. Activation speed (next business day)

- UHC/Golden Rule individual dental coverage can take effect **the day after the application is received.** From the policy provisions: "For an application sent by electronic means, the effective date will be the later of: (i) the requested effective date; or (ii) **the day after receipt by Golden Rule Insurance Company.**"
  - Source: Golden Rule dental policy document (uhone.com supply system PDF) — https://www.uhone.com/api/supplysystem/?FileName=45585B-G202108.pdf
- ⚠️ The official policy language says "the day after receipt," NOT literally "next business day." Many UHC dental plans also have **no waiting period for preventive (and on some plans basic/major) services** — coverage usable right away once active.
  - Source: UnitedHealthOne dental page — https://www.uhone.com/health-insurance/supplemental/dental-insurance (accessed 2026-06-26): "Some of our dental insurance plans offer coverage with no waiting periods for preventive, basic and most major services — you can start using them right away!"

### ✅ SAFE activation copy
> "Coverage can begin as soon as the day after your application is received (you choose your effective date), and many UHC dental plans have no waiting period for preventive care — so you can use it right away."

Avoid stating a flat "next business day" guarantee; the sourced language is "the day after receipt" + "no waiting period for preventive." Frame as "as soon as the day after."

## 6. Other authoritative, citable credibility data points (E-E-A-T boosters)

- **Plan type:** UHC-branded dental plans are **Dental PPOs** (in-network savings + out-of-network benefits). Source: https://www.uhone.com/health-insurance/supplemental/dental-insurance
- **Annual maximums:** range **$1,000 to $3,000 per year** depending on plan. Source: same uhone.com dental page (FAQ). (Cross-check actual featured-plan max against `/data/plans/` before stating on the plan page.)
- **No-waiting-period availability:** some plans cover preventive, basic, AND most major services with no waiting period (implants generally do carry a waiting period). Source: same uhone.com dental page, footnote 1.
- **Heritage / longevity:** UnitedHealthcare family of companies serving individuals & families **since 1946** (80+ years). Source: https://www.uhone.com/about-us
- **Most profitable U.S. payer:** UnitedHealth Group was the most profitable payer for full-year 2025 (~$12.05B earnings). Source: https://www.fiercehealthcare.com/payers/unitedhealth-group-was-most-profitable-payer-difficult-2025-industry — useful as a "financial stability / they'll be here when you need them" signal.
- **Standalone coverage:** UHC dental is standalone — no health plan required to buy it. Source: uhone.com dental FAQ.
- **Year-round enrollment:** dental plans available all year, no open-enrollment window required. Source: uhone.com dental page ("Are dental options available at any time? Yes.")
