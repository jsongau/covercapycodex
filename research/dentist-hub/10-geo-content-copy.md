## 4. How CoverCapy ranks offices (E-E-A-T transparency block)

Heading: How CoverCapy ranks offices

We show the full market so you can compare every option. We do not hide offices or sell the top spot. Placement reflects a small set of clear signals, in this order:

1. Distance from your search. Closer offices generally appear higher, so the results match how you would actually travel for care.
2. Patient ratings and review volume. Offices with stronger, higher-volume review signals rank above those with thinner histories within the same area.
3. PPO relevance to your plan. When you select your carrier, offices that work with that network are boosted, because in-network care is the point of your search.
4. Accreditation and availability. Verified partner offices, including Capy Accredited and Platinum Elite members, may appear higher, and may be featured within a wider radius. We label these placements so you always know what you are seeing.
5. Public listings stay visible. Unclaimed and unverified offices remain in your results so you see the whole market, not a curated few. Always confirm final PPO details directly with the office before you book.

## 6. FAQ (FAQPage schema)

**Is CoverCapy free to use?**
Yes. Searching for dentists, comparing offices, and verifying your insurance coverage are always free for patients. CoverCapy is funded by dental offices, not by the people searching for care.

**How do you verify that a dentist is in-network?**
When you select your PPO carrier, CoverCapy surfaces offices that report working with that network, and our free verify tool lets you confirm acceptance with the office before you book. We recommend confirming final PPO details directly with the office, since plan participation can change.

**Which insurance carriers does CoverCapy support?**
CoverCapy works with common PPO dental networks, including Delta Dental PPO and Premier, Cigna, MetLife, Aetna, Guardian, United Concordia, Humana, Ameritas, and others. You can filter the directory by your specific carrier to see offices that work with your plan.

**What is the difference between a PPO and a dentist's own discount plan?**
A PPO is dental insurance: your carrier negotiates set in-network rates with participating dentists, and your plan helps pay for covered care. A dentist's in-house discount plan is not insurance. It is a membership offered by a single office that gives you reduced cash prices at that office only. CoverCapy focuses on in-network PPO dentists so your existing benefits apply.

**Do you cover my state?**
Almost certainly. CoverCapy's dentist directory covers all 50 US states, including major metros and smaller cities and towns. Enter your ZIP code to see in-network offices near you.

**How fast can I be seen?**
Many offices can schedule new patients within a day or two, in keeping with our promise to get cover today and see a dentist tomorrow. Actual availability varies by office and treatment, so confirm the soonest opening directly with the office you choose. This is an estimate, not a guarantee.

**Can CoverCapy find dentists near me using my location?**
Yes. You can search by ZIP code or city, and when you allow location access, CoverCapy can detect where you are and show the closest in-network offices automatically.

**Does CoverCapy assign me to one dentist?**
No. CoverCapy is not a closed network. You see the full market of offices near you and choose the one that fits your needs, carrier, and schedule. You are never locked to a single provider.

**What if I do not have dental insurance yet?**
CoverCapy can show PPO dental plans starting around $30 per month, so you can get covered first and then book an in-network visit. Pricing is an estimate and varies by plan and location. Visit the compare plans page to see options.

**Is the information about each office accurate?**
We work to keep office details, accepted carriers, and contact information current, and we show public listings alongside verified partner offices so you see the whole market. Because plan participation and hours can change, always confirm coverage and availability with the office before your appointment.
