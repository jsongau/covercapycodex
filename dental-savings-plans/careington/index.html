The honest verdict

Careington is a strong choice when you are uninsured, have used up an insurance annual maximum, or need major work now with no waiting period. The 500 series rewards big procedures with deeper discounts, while the POS series is easier to use because more dentists accept it. As with any savings plan, confirm your dentist participates before you enroll.

What it is

Two plans, one idea

1

Careington 500 series

The deepest discounts, about 20 to 60 percent off standard fees, including 20 percent off orthodontics. The most popular plan, listed at about 12.95 dollars a month or 129 dollars a year.

2

Careington POS series

A bit less savings, about 20 to 50 percent, but accepted by more neighborhood dentists. Starts at about 9.95 dollars a month. The wider network can matter more than a few points of discount.

3

No insurance rules

No annual maximum, no waiting periods, no deductible. Usable at your first appointment, with no claims to file. You pay the discounted fee directly at the office.

4

It is not insurance

It does not pay the dentist and does not meet minimum creditable coverage. It only saves money at participating dentists, so verify yours first.

Who runs it

The engine behind the category

This is what makes Careington unusual. It is not just a plan you buy. It is the infrastructure many other brands run on.

1
A consumer brandCareington International Corporation, founded 1979 and based in Frisco, Texas, sells its own 500 and POS plans directly and through resellers like 1Dental and DentalPlans.com.
2
A wholesale administratorCareington also runs the dentist network and fee schedules behind many other carriers' branded discount plans. When you understand Careington, you understand most of the category.
3
Combo plans existSome sellers bundle Careington 500 with the Aetna Dental Access network so you always get the better discount and a larger combined pool of dentists.

Pricing

What it costs

500 series

About 12.95 dollars a month or 129 dollars a year on the official site, plus a one time 20 dollar processing fee. A 30 day refund applies, less the processing fee. Not sold in Vermont or Washington in this variant.

POS series

Starts at about 9.95 dollars a month. Accepted by more dentists, with slightly smaller discounts. Exact annual pricing varies by seller, so confirm before enrolling.

Prices vary by channel, state, and household size. The widely repeated 9.95 dollar figure applies to entry level and POS tiers. Confirm the exact plan and price at checkout.

vs a PPO

When it beats a PPO, and when it does not

Careington wins when

  • You have no insurance and want the lowest monthly cost.
  • You used up a PPO annual maximum and still need work.
  • You face a big procedure where a flat 20 to 60 percent off beats a capped benefit.
  • You need care now, with no waiting period.

A PPO wins when

  • You mainly need preventive care a PPO covers at 100 percent.
  • You can get employer subsidized premiums.
  • You expect routine, within the cap usage where insurance pays rather than discounts.

Questions

Careington FAQ

Is Careington insurance?+
No. Careington 500 and POS are dental savings plans, not insurance. You pay an annual membership fee, then pay a discounted price directly to participating dentists. There are no claims, no deductibles, no annual maximum, and no waiting periods.
How much does a Careington plan cost?+
The 500 plan is listed at about 12.95 dollars a month or 129 dollars a year, plus a one time 20 dollar processing fee, on the official site. The POS series starts at about 9.95 dollars a month. Prices vary by seller, state, and household size, so confirm the current price before enrolling.
How much can I save with Careington?+
The 500 plan advertises 20 to 60 percent off standard fees, the deepest discounts in its lineup, including 20 percent off orthodontics. The POS series saves about 20 to 50 percent but is accepted by more dentists. For example, the official site shows an adult cleaning averaging about 134 dollars dropping to about 44 dollars.
Is Careington legit?+
Careington International Corporation is a long established discount plan organization founded in 1979 and based in Frisco, Texas. It is both a consumer brand and the administrator behind many other carriers' branded discount plans. It is a legitimate discount plan, but it is not insurance and only saves money at participating dentists, so confirm your dentist participates first.
What is the difference between Careington 500 and POS?+
The 500 series gives the deeper discounts, about 20 to 60 percent, and is the most popular plan. The POS, or Point of Service, series saves a bit less, about 20 to 50 percent, but is accepted by more neighborhood dentists. The 500 trades a slightly narrower network for bigger savings.
