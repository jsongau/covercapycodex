Compare PPO Dental Plans Across Six Carriers

CoverCapy compares six PPO dental insurance plans for individuals, self-employed professionals, and families: UnitedHealthcare Primary Dental, Humana Extend 5000, Guardian Premier 2.0, Ameritas PrimeStar Care Complete, Aetna Dental Direct Preferred PPO, and Mutual of Omaha Dental Preferred. Smart Match evaluates each plan against your treatment goal (cleanings, fillings, crowns, root canals, implants, whitening, orthodontics, dentures, emergency care), timeline (need now, can wait, planning), and monthly budget. The Plan Brief modal opens a full dossier on any plan — coverage tiers, waiting periods, annual maximums, implant lifetime maximums, missing-tooth-clause language, and prior-coverage-waiver eligibility. The Verify with Dentist modal generates an in-network confirmation email to send to your dental office before you activate coverage. CoverCapy is an independent guide — we don't earn a commission on plans you activate.
