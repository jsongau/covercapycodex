Accessibility

Accessibility Statement.

Last updated: May 30, 2026

CoverCapy wants the website to be usable and accessible for as many people as possible.

Our accessibility approach

We aim to support clear navigation, readable text, keyboard-friendly pages, descriptive links, responsive layouts, and compatibility with common assistive technologies. As the website evolves, some experimental or interactive components may require additional improvement.

Feedback and assistance

If you have difficulty accessing any part of CoverCapy, or if you need information in another format, contact us at contact@covercapy.com. Please include the page URL, the issue you experienced, and the assistive technology or browser you used if relevant.

Ongoing improvements

CoverCapy is actively expanding its pages, tools, dentist discovery features, and patient portal experiences. Accessibility feedback is used to improve future releases.
