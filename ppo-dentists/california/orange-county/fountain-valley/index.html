Fountain Valley at a Glance

Finding PPO dentists in Fountain Valley, CA

Fountain Valley is a 9-square-mile city in western Orange County. The main dental corridors follow Brookhurst Street (north-south), Warner Avenue, Magnolia Avenue, and Slater Avenue.

Mile Square Regional Park anchors the northeast quarter of the city — a 640-acre green space bounded by Euclid Street, Edinger Avenue, Ward Street, and Heil Avenue. Most dental offices cluster along the city's commercial arterials rather than in residential interiors. Fountain Valley is bordered by Huntington Beach to the west and south, Westminster to the north, and Garden Grove to the northeast. Interstate 405 forms the city's southern boundary, making offices along Warner Ave and Brookhurst readily accessible from adjacent cities.
