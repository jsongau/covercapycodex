Understanding Your Plan

How Delta Dental PPO works

Delta Dental offers two main plan types — PPO and Premier. PPO plans provide higher benefits when you see a Delta Dental PPO provider, while Premier plans offer access to a large network of participating dentists. Some Delta Dental plans also include a DeltaCare HMO option.

Verify your plan tier

Not all Delta Dental plans are the same. Ask the office whether they participate in your specific Delta Dental network tier — PPO, Premier, or other — before scheduling.

Confirm current participation

PPO network contracts can change. Always call the dental office and your insurance carrier separately to verify current in-network status.

Get a written estimate

Before treatment, request a pre-authorization or written cost estimate from the office. This helps you understand your out-of-pocket responsibility under your Delta Dental plan.

CoverCapy disclaimer: CoverCapy helps patients compare and verify PPO acceptance, but final network status must be confirmed with the dental office and Delta Dental directly. Network participation can change without notice.
