About Inland Empire

Understanding the IE dental landscape

The Inland Empire — San Bernardino and Riverside Counties — is one of California's fastest-growing regions, with Chino Hills and Chino serving as anchor communities for southern San Bernardino County. The area's dental market reflects its diverse, family-oriented population with a strong mix of general, pediatric, and cosmetic practices.

PPO dental plans are common in the IE's employer base, driven by logistics, healthcare, and service-sector employers in Ontario, Chino, and surrounding cities. Delta Dental, Humana, and MetLife contracts are widespread, but individual practice participation varies by office. Saturday hours are available at several Chino Hills practices.

CoverCapy's Inland Empire directory organizes the landscape by city and carrier so patients can do their research before calling. All PPO participation must still be confirmed directly with each office and carrier.

PPO Verification Checklist

Before your first appointment at any Inland Empire dental office:

  • Confirm the office accepts your specific PPO plan tier
  • Ask about current deductible and annual maximum
  • Verify coverage percentages for your procedure type
  • Request a pre-treatment estimate for major work
  • Check that your preferred dentist (not just the practice) is in-network
Coverage details change. This checklist is informational only — always verify with your carrier and dental office directly.
