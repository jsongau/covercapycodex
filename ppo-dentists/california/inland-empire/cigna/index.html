Understanding Your Plan

How Cigna PPO works

Cigna's DPPO plans provide a large network of participating dentists with flexible out-of-network access. Cigna Dental Care (HMO) requires selection of a primary care dentist from the network. Verify which Cigna plan type you have before searching for providers.

Verify your plan tier

Not all Cigna plans are the same. Ask the office whether they participate in your specific Cigna network tier — PPO, Premier, or other — before scheduling.

Confirm current participation

PPO network contracts can change. Always call the dental office and your insurance carrier separately to verify current in-network status.

Get a written estimate

Before treatment, request a pre-authorization or written cost estimate from the office. This helps you understand your out-of-pocket responsibility under your Cigna plan.

CoverCapy disclaimer: CoverCapy helps patients compare and verify PPO acceptance, but final network status must be confirmed with the dental office and Cigna directly. Network participation can change without notice.
