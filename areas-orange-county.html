Orange County PPO Dental — FAQ

Use CoverCapy's search above to enter your ZIP code and select your PPO carrier. Verified offices show a green accepted badge. You can also request a coverage check directly from any listing before booking — no phone calls required.
Delta Dental PPO, UnitedHealthcare Dental, Guardian, MetLife, and Cigna are the most widely accepted PPO plans among Orange County practices on CoverCapy. Most Capy Accredited offices accept three or more major carriers.
Many Capy Accredited offices in Orange County offer same-week or next-day availability for new PPO patients. Look for the "Accepting New Patients" badge on the listing card, or use the New Patients filter in the search.
Capy Accredited dentists are independently reviewed PPO offices that meet CoverCapy's standards for modern equipment (digital X-rays, iTero scanners), digital workflows, and patient experience. They're ranked higher in search, verified quarterly, and can display the Capy Accredited badge to patients.
Capy Crowns are CoverCapy's loyalty currency. You earn them by buying a PPO plan through CoverCapy, booking an appointment, completing a cleaning, or referring a friend. Crowns can be redeemed for free whitening, gift cards, and office promotions at participating Orange County offices.
