Capy Accreditation is a private CoverCapy credential. It is not a government certification or a dental board license, and it does not replace either. It reflects what CoverCapy has reviewed and verified about a practice. It is not a guarantee of clinical outcomes.

Why It Exists

Patients are guessing. Capy Accreditation gives them something to verify.

Patients comparing dental offices online see the same promises everywhere. Almost every practice describes itself as gentle, modern, advanced, and caring. Those words are easy to write and difficult to confirm.

From a website alone, patients cannot easily tell:

  • Who uses modern diagnostics
  • Who invests in patient comfort
  • Who uses digital scanning
  • Who communicates treatment clearly
  • Who documents care thoroughly
  • Who is set up for a smooth, concierge style experience

Capy Accreditation replaces vague claims with verified signals. When an office is Capy Accredited, CoverCapy has reviewed and confirmed specific markers of modern, patient first care.

What The Review Looks At

What the Capy review looks at

Each accreditation decision considers the same areas.

Patient Trust

Consistent, positive patient sentiment across major public review and health platforms.

Established practices are expected to show strong, steady patient satisfaction over time, not a handful of recent reviews.

Modern Diagnostics

CBCT or 3D imaging and digital scanning, such as iTero or an equivalent, are strongly preferred.

Modern imaging and patient education workflows help offices diagnose accurately and explain treatment clearly.

Clinical Standards

Active licensure in good standing, ethical treatment planning, clear documentation, and patient first decisions.

CoverCapy confirms active, in good standing licensure and looks for transparent, patient first treatment practices.

Patient Experience

Respectful scheduling, transparent expectations, comfort focused visits, and organized follow up.

A low friction patient journey, from first call to follow up, is part of what the review considers.

Technology Investment

Digital workflows, modern ultrasonic scaling systems, comfort improving tools, and current imaging.

Capy Accreditation favors modern ultrasonic and digital workflows. Offices that rely heavily on outdated systems may be asked to modernize before approval.

Organized Benefit Workflows

PPO-ready systems and coverage-aware treatment planning, so benefits are organized and used effectively.

Capy Accredited offices run organized benefit workflows: coverage-aware treatment planning, clear cost conversations, and patient financing readiness, with PPO plans handled smoothly in the background.

Trust only works when standards are maintained.

Capy Accreditation is maintained annually. Credentials are reviewed for continued alignment with the standard, because a private mark only holds meaning when its quality control is ongoing.

Questions

Capy Accreditation, answered

What is Capy Accreditation?+
Capy Accreditation is a private quality credential from CoverCapy. It reflects CoverCapy's verification of specific markers of modern, patient first care. It is not a government certification or a dental board license.
What is a Capy Accredited Dentist?+
A Capy Accredited Dentist is a practice reviewed by CoverCapy against the Capy Accreditation standard for modern, technology forward, patient first care. It is a private CoverCapy credential, not a government certification or a dental board license, and it helps patients find a CoverCapy dentist that has been reviewed.
Is Capy Accreditation a good fit for PPO dentists?+
Yes. Capy Accreditation suits PPO dentists who run organized, coverage aware benefit workflows. A Capy Accredited Dentist plans treatment around your PPO plan and in network rates so your coverage is used effectively. Accreditation is reviewed and is not guaranteed by payment alone.
Is accreditation guaranteed if I pay?+
No. Membership begins a review process. Payment does not activate the credential. Offices can be conditionally approved, asked to improve first, or declined.
Do Capy Accredited offices accept PPO dental insurance?+
Yes. Capy Accredited offices accept PPO dental insurance and treatment plan around your in network rates and plan benefits, so your coverage is used effectively.
What is a modern dental practice?+
A modern dental practice uses current technology and digital workflows, such as 3D imaging, digital scanning, and clear digital treatment planning, alongside organized patient communication. Capy Accreditation reviews offices against this modern dentistry standard.
Can a new dental office qualify?+
Yes. Newer offices may qualify by demonstrating exceptional technology, diagnostics, and patient experience systems, even without a long review history.
What technology is expected?+
Modern diagnostics and digital workflows are expected, including CBCT or 3D imaging, digital scanning, ultrasonic scaling, digital imaging, and organized patient communication. Offices relying on outdated workflows may need to modernize before approval.
Do I need iTero?+
For standard accreditation, iTero or an equivalent scanner is strongly preferred. For Platinum Elite, iTero is required without exception.
What is Platinum Elite?+
Platinum Elite is the highest and most selective level of Capy Accreditation, reserved for offices with advanced technology, proven patient experience, and the infrastructure to support premium CoverCapy programs. It is reviewed and limited, not available by payment alone.
Can I rank higher in my city?+
Accredited offices are eligible for a clearly labeled Featured Member placement in CoverCapy search. Where an office appears is influenced by accreditation status, profile completeness, local availability, patient trust indicators, and participation. CoverCapy does not promise a specific position or a number of patients.
What happens if my office does not qualify?+
An office may be conditionally approved, asked to improve specific areas first, or declined. CoverCapy outlines what can be addressed before reapplying.
Is this the same as a dental board license?+
No. Capy Accreditation is not a dental license and does not replace state dental board requirements. It is a private CoverCapy ecosystem credential designed to help patients identify reviewed, technology forward, patient trusted offices.
