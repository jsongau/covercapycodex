Insurance Disclaimer

Insurance Disclaimer.

Last updated: May 30, 2026

CoverCapy helps users understand PPO dental plan concepts, treatment cost estimates, and dentist discovery. CoverCapy is not a dental insurance carrier and does not make final benefit determinations.

Not an insurance carrier

CoverCapy does not underwrite insurance, issue insurance policies, pay claims, approve benefits, or guarantee plan enrollment. Any insurance-related information shown on CoverCapy is educational and should be verified with the carrier, plan administrator, employer benefits administrator, broker, or dental office.

Benefits vary

Dental plan benefits vary by carrier, plan, state, eligibility, network status, waiting period, annual maximum, deductible, frequency limitation, procedure code, provider contract, and treatment history.

Estimates are not final bills

Treatment cost estimates are not final quotes, pre-authorizations, explanations of benefits, or guarantees. Actual costs may differ based on diagnosis, procedure codes, materials, provider fees, complications, add-on services, financing terms, and final insurance processing.

Verify before enrollment or treatment

Before enrolling in a plan or beginning treatment, users should confirm benefits, eligibility, waiting periods, provider participation, pre-authorization requirements, treatment plans, and final costs directly with the insurance carrier and dental office.

No dental advice

CoverCapy does not diagnose, treat, or recommend a specific clinical course of care. Clinical decisions should be made between a patient and a licensed dental professional.
