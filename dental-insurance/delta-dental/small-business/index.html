Inside Delta Dental · small business

Delta Dental for small business

A small team can offer Delta group dental with no waiting periods and the largest dentist network in the country, so employees across many ZIP codes are likely already in network.

How does Delta Dental work for a small business?
A small business, typically 2 or more employees, can offer Delta group dental in three shapes: a PPO, PPO Plus Premier (a dual network), or the DeltaCare USA HMO. Group plans have no waiting periods at any size, employer-selectable annual maximums, commonly $1,000 to $3,000, some with a carryover that grows the maximum over claim-light years, and clear aligners at no added cost. The pitch is reach: most dentists already take Delta. Rates are quoted per group. CoverCapy sells neither insurance nor plans.
Delta Dental group plans have no waiting periods at any group size, and Delta runs the largest dentist network in the country. Source: Delta Dental Small Business Program, verified 2026.

Cost

What a small-business plan costs

Group rates are quoted per business and pooled or community rated for small groups, so there is no single national price. As an illustration only, one state entity, Delta Dental of Connecticut, lists employee-only PPO rates starting around $28 to $38 a month depending on the design. Your rate depends on location, plan choice and group size. Employers pick the annual maximum from a menu, commonly $1,000 to $3,000, and can add a carryover on some designs.

The Connecticut figures are a labeled example, not a national rate. Get a quote for your business, ZIP and group size.

Why Delta

Why Delta for a small team

The strongest single line for a business owner whose staff are spread across ZIP codes is that most offices already take Delta, so almost everyone can stay with a dentist they know. Group plans also carry no waiting periods at any size, include clear aligners at no extra cost, and offer a carryover on some designs that can grow the effective maximum over claim-light years.

Common questions

What is the smallest business that can get Delta group dental?
Typically 2 employees. The small-business program generally serves groups of 2 to 99, though the exact range varies by state and Delta company, for example up to 150 in Illinois.
Do Delta Dental group plans have waiting periods?
No. Delta group plans have no waiting periods at any group size, which is different from individual plans that usually wait 6 to 12 months on basic and major work.
How much does small-business Delta dental cost?
Rates are quoted per group and pooled or community rated, so there is no national price. As a labeled example, one state entity lists employee-only PPO rates starting around $28 to $38 a month. Employers choose the annual maximum, commonly $1,000 to $3,000.
