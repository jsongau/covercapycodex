UC SHIP dental FAQ

Quick answers

Do I need a referral?
No. Choose a Delta Dental PPO dentist, give the office your Enrollee ID, and book directly.
What will it cost me?
Preventive cleanings and exams are covered. Other work is shared after a small deductible, up to an annual maximum commonly around $1,000 per plan year. Confirm your campus and year's exact terms at the carrier.
I'm graduating or losing UC SHIP, what then?
When student coverage ends, you can keep Delta with an individual plan. See the Delta Dental PPO Individual Premium Plan for coverage that continues after graduation.
