On SCAN in CA or WA? Your dental is Delta Dental.

If you're on a SCAN Health Plan Medicare Advantage plan in California or Washington, your dental coverage runs through Delta Dental, a DeltaCare USA network benefit, with an optional PPO buy‑up for more dentist choice. Here's what you have and how to use it before you ever pay for a separate plan.

Does SCAN Health Plan include Delta Dental?
Yes, in California and Washington. SCAN provides dental through Delta Dental for CA & WA members. Most members use the DeltaCare USA (HMO) network, and some plans offer an optional PPO buy‑up (the Delta Dental Medicare Advantage PPO network) for an extra monthly premium and broader dentist choice. SCAN members in AZ, NV, NM and TX get dental through DentaQuest instead. Check the back of your 2026 member ID card.

Good to know

SCAN Delta Dental FAQs

Is the SCAN Delta Dental benefit a PPO?
By default it's a DeltaCare USA HMO benefit, where you use an assigned in‑network office with set copays. Some SCAN plans let you add an optional PPO buy‑up for an extra premium, which opens up the broader Delta Dental Medicare Advantage PPO network.
Which states does the SCAN Delta benefit cover?
California and Washington. SCAN members in Arizona, Nevada, New Mexico and Texas get dental through DentaQuest instead.
How do I find a dentist?
Search deltadentalins.com/scan or call Delta Dental at 1‑855‑830‑6583 (TTY 711). Or have CoverCapy find and book one for you.
Should I also buy an individual Delta PPO?
Usually not if your SCAN dental already meets your needs, since you'd be paying twice. Consider an individual Delta PPO only if you're not Medicare‑eligible, not on SCAN, or want coverage your SCAN benefit doesn't include. These are separate products with separate rules.
