Dental insurance in one minute

Individual dental insurance comes mainly in two shapes: PPO plans (see any dentist, pay less in network, higher premiums, waiting periods on major work) and HMO plans (lower premiums, assigned network dentist, no out-of-network coverage).

PPO plans typically cover preventive care at 100% from day one; basic and major care follow coinsurance schedules and waiting periods that vary widely between plans — the differences decide thousands of dollars on crowns and implants.
