Frequently asked

Questions from practices.

Is claiming my profile free? +
Yes. Qualified practices can claim a free CoverCapy profile so patients can verify practice info, PPO acceptance, photos, reviews, and booking. Free profiles are visible but don't receive the same placement priority as Featured or Platinum offices.
What does Capy Featured do? +
$369/month, or $295/month billed annually (save 20%) with a Founder's Rate Lock that holds your preferred pricing as an active member. You get Pre-Verified PPO Readiness, insurance-aware intake support, preferred placement when relevance is equal, treatment and cosmetic visibility, a financing-friendly indicator, and priority inclusion on treatment pages. Cancel anytime.
What is Platinum Elite? +
Platinum Elite is our most selective tier, starting at $1,499/month and available by application only to active Capy Featured practices. We review PPO workflow, treatment-category fit, ratings, and market availability — and intentionally limit placements per city to preserve quality and balance. Acceptance and availability vary by market.
Does CoverCapy guarantee patients? +
No. CoverCapy doesn't guarantee a specific number of leads, appointments, or revenue. We improve discovery, trust signals, patient education, and booking — so qualified practices can compete for PPO-ready patients more effectively.
Are CoverCapy bookings unlimited? +
Yes. Featured and Platinum memberships include unlimited PPO patient bookings — CoverCapy does not charge per-patient or per-appointment fees on top of membership.
How do I get listed higher in my city? +
Start by claiming free. Complete your profile, PPO plans, photos, services. Then activate Featured to outrank free listings, or start Platinum vetting for top placement.
