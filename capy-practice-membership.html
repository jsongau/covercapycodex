FAQ

Questions dentists will ask before joining.

Can I claim my CoverCapy dentist profile for free?
Yes. The free tier is for correcting and controlling basic listing information like practice name, address, phone number, active dentist, and core profile details.
Who should apply for Capy Featured?
Capy Featured is for practices that are confident in their clinical skills, patient experience, and ability to handle PPO-aware conversations. It is meant to help strong offices stand out without claiming they are Platinum Elite.
Who should apply for Platinum Elite?
Platinum Elite is best for offices with systems in place to handle an influx of new patients. It is especially fitting for practices with multiple providers, growth plans, or multiple locations.
Is this only for Southern California?
No. CoverCapy is being built nationwide, so the membership page is structured for city, state, metro, treatment, and practice-profile expansion across the country.
Can patients request a dentist who is not yet on CoverCapy?
Yes. Patient demand can help identify which offices should be invited into the network, especially for treatment-specific pages like implants, Invisalign, whitening, crowns, and cosmetic dentistry.
Can this page connect to a real dentist portal later?
Yes. The buttons and forms are structured so you can later connect them to Supabase, a CRM, an application workflow, or separate pages like apply-capy-practice-membership.html.
