What it is

A paid, labeled spot in a directory patients already trust

CoverCapy is a concierge PPO dental platform with a directory spanning thousands of city, metro, and state pages. A sponsored listing gives your practice a Featured Member position inside that directory, so the patients who arrive through the PPO funnel see you sooner.

It is not a different listing from your real profile. It is your verified profile, given more prominence, with the Capy Accredited badge shown where your office has earned it. Sponsorship raises visibility. It does not invent a rating or hide an advertisement.

How visibility works

How ranking and visibility are decided

Sponsorship raises your floor, but where you land is still earned. Several signals shape placement.

  • Membership tier and its search radius: free reaches 8 miles, Capy Accredited 10 miles, Platinum Elite 18 miles.
  • Profile completeness: networks accepted, treatments, photos, hours, and verified details.
  • Local availability: whether your office is open to new patients in that area.
  • Patient trust indicators: consistent, positive public sentiment over time.
  • Participation: how actively your office responds to patient interest.
  • Capy Crowns score: the tiebreaker when offices are otherwise closely matched.

CoverCapy does not promise a specific position or a number of patients. Sponsorship buys greater visibility, and the rest is earned through a strong, verified profile.

C

Every sponsored slot is labeled. Featured Member and sponsored placements are clearly identified in CoverCapy search and are governed by our Advertising Disclosure. Sponsorship buys visibility only.
