Dental billing specialist and editorial reviewer for CoverCapy.

What J Song reviews

J Song is a dental billing specialist who reviews CoverCapy's PPO dental insurance content for billing and coverage accuracy. The focus is the part patients get wrong most often: how a plan actually pays a claim.

On each plan, glossary, and treatment-cost page, that review checks that coinsurance is described against the plan's allowed amount rather than the dentist's full fee, that the deductible is applied in the right order, that annual maximums are presented as a limit on what the plan pays, and that waiting periods and Year 1 versus Year 2 coverage match how carriers adjudicate real claims. The goal is simple: no figure on CoverCapy should overstate what a plan will actually pay.

How the review works

Content is checked against carrier benefit documents and standardized CDT procedure codes before publication, and re-checked when a plan's benefits change. Where a number is a typical range rather than a fixed rule, the page says so and points readers to confirm the exact figure with the carrier.
