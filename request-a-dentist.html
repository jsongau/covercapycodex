Questions, answered

How do I request a dentist on CoverCapy?+
Fill out the Request a Dentist form with your name, email, and city or ZIP. Add the practice name if you have one, and your PPO carrier if you know it. CoverCapy's concierge then verifies coverage or finds in-network options and replies to you, free of charge.
Can CoverCapy add my current dentist?+
Yes. Enter your dentist's practice name and city and CoverCapy will check whether the office is in network or can join CoverCapy, then confirm what your PPO plan covers there. There is no cost to ask.
Do I have to give my insurance member ID?+
No. The Request a Dentist form never asks for your member ID, group number, or Social Security number. You only share your name, contact details, location, and optionally a carrier name, so CoverCapy can route your request.
Is requesting a dentist free?+
Yes. Submitting a request and getting concierge help to find or verify a PPO dentist is free. CoverCapy makes money through optional membership plans and accredited practices, not by charging patients to ask for help.
How fast will CoverCapy respond?+
CoverCapy aims to reply by email within one business day. If you add a phone number, the concierge may call with options. Response time can vary with carrier confirmations and office availability in your area.
What if I do not have insurance yet?+
Choose "I do not have insurance yet" as your carrier and CoverCapy can show PPO dental plans that start fast, then help you find an in-network dentist. You can compare plans first on the Compare PPO Dental Plans page.
