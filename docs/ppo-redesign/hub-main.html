Methodology

How we choose, and how we make money

CoverCapy is independent. No carrier pays for placement; offices in our network pay us, not the plan you pick. Every plan fact is traceable to an official source with a last-verified date. Premiums are illustrative and vary by state, age, and ZIP.

FAQ

PPO dental insurance, answered

Which plan has no waiting period for major work? Mutual of Omaha and Ameritas cover major from day one (20%, rising to 50% in Year 2). Cheapest plan? UnitedHealthcare Primary Dental, from $30/mo. Adult braces? Delta PPO Premium is one of the only individual PPOs that covers them.
