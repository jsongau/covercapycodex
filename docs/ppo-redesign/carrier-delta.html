Best-fit treatments

What Delta is good for

Adult braces: Delta PPO Premium is one of the few individual plans that covers them. Broadest dentist choice: the combined PPO + Premier network. Flexible start date: the 1st-or-15th activation is unique. For day-one major work, compare Mutual of Omaha or Ameritas instead.

FAQs

Common Delta questions

Does Delta cover adult braces? Yes — PPO Premium, 50% after a 12-month wait, one of the only individual PPOs that does. PPO or Premier? PPO is cheaper out-of-pocket; Premier has more dentists at a higher cost. When does it start? The 1st or 15th of the month — your choice.
