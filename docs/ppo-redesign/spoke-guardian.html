Premier 2.0

One of the only individual PPOs that pays 85% on basic work from day one — no wait on fillings — plus children's orthodontics, all behind an A++ rated carrier. The trade-off is a 12-month wait on major work.

Waiting periods & timing

When each tier becomes usable

  • Day 1 — preventive (100%) and basic (85%) are live immediately. Coverage itself begins the 1st of the month after you enrol.
  • Month 12 — major work (crowns, root canals) and child orthodontics reach 50%.
  • Prior coverage — 12 months of comparable prior coverage can waive the waits. Note a 12-month re-enrolment lockout after cancellation.
A realistic first year

Example: a filling now, a crown later

Two cleanings (covered 100%) and a $250 filling in month 2 (85% → you pay ~$38 after the deductible). A $1,400 crown is best timed after month 12, when major reaches 50%. This plan rewards "basic now, major planned."

Fine print

The clauses that move your bill

  • Missing-tooth clause applies to teeth lost before the effective date.
  • Annual maximum grows with tenure on lower tiers; Premier 2.0 carries the $3,000 ceiling.
  • Adult orthodontics not covered on any Guardian individual plan.
  • Re-enrolment lockout of 12 months after cancellation — don't cancel without a replacement.
  • Whitening is a Diamond-tier feature; confirm before assuming it on Premier 2.0.
  • Out-of-network reimbursed against Guardian's allowed amount.
