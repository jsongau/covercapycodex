## The three plans this build uses

### UHC Primary Dental: the Care Now maintenance plan
| Field | Value (modeled) |
|---|---|
| Premium | about 30 dollars a month |
| Annual maximum | about 1,000 dollars |
| Deductible | about 50 dollars |
| Cleaning and exam | covered in full, preventive first |
| Fillings | about 50 percent, from day one, no wait |
| Root canal | not covered |
| Crown | not covered |
| Best for | someone who needs a dentist now and mostly needs maintenance |

Use it when: care cannot wait and the need is cleaning and fillings. Max the small maximum on what it covers, finance anything larger.

### Ameritas PrimeStar: the Care Now bridge for urgent major work
| Field | Value (modeled) |
|---|---|
| Premium | about 60 dollars a month |
| Annual maximum | about 2,000 dollars |
| Deductible | about 50 dollars |
| Cleaning and exam | covered in full, off the maximum |
| Fillings | about 80 percent, from day one |
| Root canal | about 80 percent, from day one |
| Crown | about 20 percent in year one, from day one |
| Best for | someone who needs major work now and will not wait six months |

Use it when: the crown or root canal cannot wait and the person still wants the plan to pay part of it immediately. It pays something on major work from day one, which UHC Primary does not.

### Humana Extend 5000: the Strategize plan
| Field | Value (modeled) |
|---|---|
| Premium | about 100 dollars a month |
| Annual maximum | about 5,000 dollars |
| Deductible | about 75 dollars |
| Cleaning and exam | covered in full |
| Fillings | about 80 percent, after a three-month wait |
| Root canal | about 50 percent, after a six-month wait |
| Crown | about 50 percent, after a six-month wait |
| Best for | a real treatment plan ahead, with the patience to wait six months |

Use it when: there is significant major work and the person can wait. The 5,000 dollar maximum and the 50 percent major coverage pay far more than a small plan once the waiting period clears.

## The pathway-to-plan map

| Situation | Plan | What the plan does | What financing does |
|---|---|---|---|
| Care now, maintenance | UHC Primary Dental | Pays cleaning in full, fillings at about half, now | Carries the rest of any larger work at 0% |
| Care now, urgent major work | Ameritas PrimeStar | Pays part of the crown or root canal from day one | Carries the remaining balance at 0% |
| Plan ahead, lots of work | Humana Extend 5000 | After the six-month wait, pays about half of crowns up to 5,000 dollars | Carries the remaining copay at 0% |
| Plan ahead, very large case | Humana Extend 5000 plus phasing | Two annual maximums across the reset | Carries whatever copay remains at 0% |

## Honesty and compliance notes

- A low maximum is the right plan for someone who needs care now and does not need major coverage. Never call it bad. Frame it as fit.
- A six-month wait is a feature of the strategize path, not a flaw. Frame it as the price of a much larger maximum.
- Do not promise a specific reimbursement. Say the plan covers about half of major work after the waiting period, subject to the maximum, the deductible, and plan terms.
- Confirm participation and exact terms with the carrier and the dental office. The carrier name alone does not confirm network participation.
- 0% APR is for eligible patients where offered. CoverCapy is not a lender. Approval and terms vary.
