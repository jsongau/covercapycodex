How we handle your message

The concierge reads every message and routes it to the right desk. To help us respond faster, include the relevant page URL and a clear description of what you need. For listing questions, name the practice and city so we can find the right profile.

Privacy, accessibility, legal

For privacy, accessibility, or legal notices

Email contact@covercapy.com with the relevant page URL and a clear description of your request so we can route it properly. You can also review our privacy policy, accessibility statement, and terms of service.

Emergency notice

CoverCapy does not provide emergency dental or medical care. For urgent symptoms, contact a licensed dental professional, urgent care provider, or emergency services as appropriate.
