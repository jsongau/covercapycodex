What is the CoverCapy rating?

The CoverCapy rating is a 0-to-100 composite score computed for each dental plan and dentist profile on the platform, synthesizing four weighted factors: coverage breadth (what CDT codes are covered and at what coinsurance), annual maximum relative to premium, waiting period structure (shorter waits score higher), and verified patient review quality. The rating is designed to let patients compare plans beyond the surface-level sticker price, a $25/month plan with a 12-month major wait and a $1,000 max may score lower than a $38/month plan with a 6-month wait and a $1,500 max, even though the cheap plan's marketing appears more attractive.

How it works

CoverCapy's scoring algorithm weights each factor by its impact on patient out-of-pocket costs in a typical dental care scenario. Coverage breadth is assessed against a standard care basket (two cleanings, one set of X-rays, one filling, one crown per 5 years). Annual maximum is compared to average plan-year utilization. Waiting periods are penalized using a cost-of-delay model (months of delayed care times estimated procedure cost). Review quality draws from verified appointment-based ratings on the CoverCapy platform.

Plan A: $22/month, $1,000 max, 12-month major wait, 80/50 coinsurance. CoverCapy score: 61. Plan B: $36/month, $1,500 max, 6-month basic / 12-month major wait, 80/50 coinsurance. CoverCapy score: 74. Plan B scores higher despite costing $14/month more because the higher annual maximum and shorter basic waiting period produce better expected outcomes for a patient who uses their benefits.

What to watch out for

  • ⚠The CoverCapy rating is a comparative tool, not a guarantee of plan performance. Individual situations, your specific dental needs, location, preferred dentist's network participation, may cause the right plan for you to differ from the highest-rated plan.
  • ⚠Ratings are recalculated as plan benefits, premiums, and review data update. A plan's score may change at annual renewal. Re-verify your plan's rating when shopping each year.
