# 11 — Multi-Carrier Savings Plan Landscape

The map for building the next pages after Aetna Dental Access. The strategic insight: the dental *discount* market is a branding layer over two networks — **Aetna Dental Access** and **Careington**. Most "carrier" plans license one of them rather than run their own. That means per-carrier pages can be largely templated; the differentiators are brand, network, price, and the "who actually runs it" story.

> Every "discount/savings" plan below is **NOT insurance** — no claims, deductibles, annual maximums, or waiting periods; the member pays the discounted rate directly at the chair.

---

## The two backbones

1. **Aetna Dental Access** — network administered by Aetna Life Insurance Company. The single largest discount-dental network; licensed by many other brands.
2. **Careington** — Careington International Corp. (Frisco, TX). Both a consumer brand (Careington 500, POS) and the **administrator (DPO) behind many other carriers' branded plans.** Understand Careington and you understand most of the category.

## Traps to avoid on future pages

- **Delta Dental trap:** *DeltaCare USA* sounds like a discount plan but is **DHMO insurance.** The actual discount product is **Delta Dental Patient Direct.** Easy and common mistake.
- **Humana / UHC trap:** each sells *both* insurance (Humana Extend/Preventive Value/Complete; UHC PPO) *and* a separate discount plan. Keep them distinct on the page.
- **"Carrier" ≠ administrator:** Humana's discount plan is really Careington; saying so is a strong, original GEO angle ("who actually runs these plans?") that competitors avoid.

## Recommended page build order (after Aetna Dental Access)

1. **Careington 500** — the backbone brand; explaining it illuminates the whole category.
2. **Humana Dental Savings Plus** — high search volume; the Careington-administered angle is a content hook.
3. **Cigna CignaPlus Savings** — large own network, distinct.
4. **UnitedHealthcare Dental Savings Complete** — in-house network, ties to CoverCapy's existing UHC PPO content.
5. **Delta Dental Patient Direct** — strongest brand; the DeltaCare confusion is worth owning.
6. **MetLife Discount Dental Program** — clearest tiered pricing; affinity distribution angle.

Each reuses the `10-master-content-brief.md` structure. The shared components — dentist economics (`00`), savings-vs-PPO framework (`05`), scam/myth-buster, and the verification/decision CTAs — carry across all of them.
