# 04 — Pricing Analysis

What it costs, where the prices diverge, and what must be re-verified before any number goes on a live page.

---

## The key pricing fact: same network, different price by retailer

Because the Aetna Dental Access *network* is licensed and resold by multiple discount plan organizations, **the same underlying plan is sold at different prices depending on the marketplace.** This is the most important thing to understand about pricing, and a genuine consumer-protection angle CoverCapy can own: the dentist network and discounts are identical, but what you pay for the membership is not.

---

## Verified pricing (as of June 2026)

**DentalPlans.com — administered by New Benefits, Ltd.** *(verified May 7, 2026 / fetched June 26, 2026)*
- Individual: **$154.95/year** (about $12.91/month equivalent, billed annually)
- Family: **$209.95/year** (individual $154.95 + $55 family upgrade; about $17.50/month equivalent)
- One-time processing fee: **$20 at checkout**
- First-year individual cost all-in: **~$174.95**

**1Dental — administered by Careington International** *(fetched June 26, 2026)*
- 1 member: **$175/year** (about $14.58/month, billed annually), all fees included
- 2-member and 3+-member tiers offered, but exact figures were not shown numerically on the fetched page — **[UNCERTAIN], confirm before publishing**

**Careington-direct lower tiers** *(from search snippets only — treat as approximate, re-verify)*
- Individual ~$6.95/month or ~$75/year; member+1 ~$11.95/month or ~$129/year; family ~$15.95/month or ~$172/year — **[UNCERTAIN], not confirmed from a directly fetched primary page**

---

## Reconciling the spread

A reasonable, defensible summary for the page:

> At the two major retailers we verified, an individual Aetna Dental Access membership runs about **$155–$175 per year**, and a family membership runs about **$210 per year**, plus a one-time processing fee of around $20 at some sellers. Lower-cost tiers (roughly $75–$172/year) are advertised through some Careington channels, but pricing varies by seller and promotion — always confirm the current price at checkout.

Do not publish a single hard number as "the price." Publish the verified range and date it.

---

## Membership fee vs procedure cost (the framing that matters)

The membership fee is small relative to a single major procedure, and that's the entire value calculation:

- First-year individual cost (DentalPlans.com): **~$175.**
- Savings on one molar root canal alone: **~$744** ($1,535 → $791).
- Savings on one porcelain crown alone: **~$551** ($1,500 → $949).

A single major procedure pays back the full-year membership several times over. For someone facing major work, the plan is almost always net-positive. For someone who only needs two cleanings a year, the math is much tighter — the page must say so honestly (covered in `05` and `07`).

---

## Regional and promotional notes

- **Membership price appears flat nationally per retailer** — there was no ZIP-based membership pricing observed. (The *dentist fee schedules* vary by ZIP, but the *membership fee* does not.)
- **Promotions vary and carry restrictions** — e.g., some "free months" offers are explicitly not available to California residents.
- **State exclusions:** not available in Vermont or Washington (per 1Dental/Careington).

---

## Cancellation and renewal

- **30-day cancellation window** with a full refund of membership fees if cancelled within the first 30 days after the effective date.
  - UT, AR, TN: full refund of all fees within 30 days.
  - MD: membership fee plus one-time application fee (minus $5) refunded within 30 days on return of the discount card.
- **Renewal:** sold as annual memberships billed annually (monthly options are billed-annually equivalents). **[UNCERTAIN] exact auto-renewal / auto-bill mechanics** were not stated on the pages fetched — confirm before describing renewal behavior, since billing/auto-renewal is one of the category's most common complaint sources.
