## The three rules for using this research

1. **Discount plan, NOT insurance.** Every document and every page must state this plainly. No claims, no deductibles, no annual maximums, no waiting periods. The member pays the discounted fee directly to the dentist at the visit.
2. **Never publish an `[UNCERTAIN]` number.** Prices, network size, and a few mechanics vary by retailer and date. Confirm at the cited source before it goes live.
3. **PPO facts come only from `/data/plans/`.** Per the repo's CLAUDE.md rule 13, any comparison to a CoverCapy featured PPO plan must pull its numbers from the `/data/plans/` SSOT, not from this research folder.

## The strategic thesis (one paragraph)

The entire dental-savings-plan internet is a monoculture: carriers and affiliate sites publish the same "what is it / no waiting period / no annual max / save ~50%" explainer, then funnel to enroll. The two things a real person needs to decide — **honest disqualifiers** ("when NOT to buy") and **transparent dollar math** — are precisely what affiliates and carriers are structurally disincentivized to publish. CoverCapy can own three gaps no competitor will touch: the **dentist-economics "why your dentist says yes" story**, honest **when-not-to-buy guidance**, and **real worked math** — all reinforced by CoverCapy's per-dentist verification, the `/find-my-dentist` directory, and the sourced-and-dated `/data/plans/` SSOT.
