## Positioning and notes

Delta Dental is one of the FEDVIP carriers, offering a Standard and a High PPO plan to federal employees, annuitants, and the military-retiree community (retired service members, gray-area reservists, and their families). The standout benefit is the High Plan's unlimited in-network annual maximum, which almost no private individual plan offers, plus adult orthodontics up to a $3,500 child / $2,000 adult lifetime maximum.

The key scoping point for the military audience: FEDVIP is for retirees, not active-duty families. Active-duty family dental is the separate TRICARE Dental Program administered by United Concordia. A CoverCapy federal page should welcome military retirees to Delta FEDVIP and point active-duty families to TRICARE, never imply Delta runs TRICARE.
