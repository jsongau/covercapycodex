## Positioning and notes

DeltaCare USA is the trade opposite of a PPO. You give up dentist choice (you use one assigned network general dentist, specialists by referral) and in return you get predictable set copays, no deductible, no waiting periods, and no annual dollar maximum. For someone who needs a lot of work in one year and does not mind using an assigned dentist, "no annual maximum" is the headline: the plan does not stop paying at $1,000 or $2,000 the way a PPO does.

The honest limits: the DHMO network is much smaller than Delta's PPO and Premier networks, out-of-network care is not covered except emergencies, and copays are state-specific, so the same crown can carry a different copay in California than in Texas. Premium is low but only quoted by state and ZIP, and the individual plan is sold in just nine states plus DC.
