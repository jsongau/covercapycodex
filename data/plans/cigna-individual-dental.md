## Positioning and notes
Cigna's individual line completes our Cigna coverage (we already have its group file). It is a clean, year-round-enrollable, recognizable-brand option with published average premiums (about $19-$44/mo for a single person). The standout for families is Cigna Dental 1500, the only individual plan that covers orthodontia, and notably it covers adults as well as children (50%, $1,000 lifetime) rather than restricting to under-19 like many competitors.

The structural quirks to communicate honestly: implants are excluded on every dental-only plan and appear only on the bundled Dental Vision Hearing 3500; and the high-max Dental 3000/100 pays a price for its ceiling, applying its $100 deductible to preventive and covering basic at only 50%. Standard waits are 6 months basic / 12 months major, waivable with 12 months of prior major-inclusive coverage (ortho and implant waits are never waived).

Dentist value angle: a recognizable national DPPO with two network tiers (Advantage steers to deeper-discount dentists); the adults-allowed ortho on the 1500 plan is a differentiator for adult orthodontic cases.
