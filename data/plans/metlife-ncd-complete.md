## Positioning and notes
NCD Complete is the highest annual maximum we track ($10,000). Its edge is the high ceiling plus a one-time $100 lifetime deductible and no waiting periods. Its weakness is year-one major coverage at only ~10%, so it rewards people who can time bigger work (implant plus crowns) for year two or three, when major reaches 50 to 60 percent. Compared with Humana Extend 5000: NCD has double the maximum, but Humana pays 50% on major after a 6-month wait (better early-year reimbursement) and has a higher dedicated lifetime implant allowance for people doing two or more implants. Runs on MetLife PDP Plus, one of the largest networks. Pages: dental-insurance/ppo-plans/metlife-ncd-complete/ and the MetLife find-a-dentist page.
