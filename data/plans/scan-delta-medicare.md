## Positioning and notes

SCAN is the clearest real-world example of the HMO-versus-PPO choice for someone on Medicare. The dental benefit included at no extra premium runs on the DeltaCare USA DHMO network: you use an assigned network dentist and pay set copays, with no annual dollar maximum on covered services. If you want to keep your own dentist, SCAN sells an Optional Dental PPO Buy-Up for an added premium that moves you to the Delta Dental Medicare Advantage PPO network and lets you see any provider.

That is the same trade the individual market offers, one layer up: the standard plan is the low-cost DHMO with an assigned dentist, and the buy-up is the pay-more-for-freedom PPO. It is why the deltacare-hmo-vs-ppo page uses SCAN as its Medicare bridge.

This file supports the over-65 and deltacare-hmo-vs-ppo pages. Specific 2026 copays and allowances belong in the member's Evidence of Coverage, not on the page. Pair with deltacare-usa.md (the DHMO mechanics) and delta-dental-ppo-premium.md (the PPO mechanics).
