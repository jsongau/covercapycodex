## Positioning and notes
Aetna Dental Direct is the balanced, everyday PPO on the shelf. It follows the standard 100/80/50 structure: preventive at 100% from day one, basic at 80% after a 6-month wait, and major work (crowns, bridges, dentures) at 50% after a 12-month wait, under a modest ~$1,250 annual maximum. Its standout feature is the network, one of the largest dental PPO networks in the country at 442,000+ dentist locations, where in-network dentists are paid directly so members do not file their own claims. Its second edge is waivability: both the 6-month and 12-month waits can be waived with proof of prior continuous dental coverage, which makes it strongest for people coming straight off an employer dental plan who want basic and major work eligible sooner. It does not cover implants or orthodontics, and the ~$1,250 ceiling will be brushed by two crowns in one year, so it is positioned as balanced everyday coverage rather than a high-ceiling plan. It carries a missing tooth clause. Page: dental-insurance/ppo-plans/aetna-dental-direct/.
