## Positioning and notes
Delta Dental PPO Premium is the network-and-orthodontics play. Its two standout features are the largest dentist network in the U.S. (112,000+ dentists at 278,000+ locations on the PPO network, the largest by location count) and adult orthodontics, which most individual PPOs leave out, both adults and children at 50% up to a $1,500 lifetime maximum. It also offers unusually flexible 1st or 15th-of-month start dates.

The trade-off is longer waits and a mid-size ceiling. Major work, implants and orthodontics each wait a full 12 months, the longest on the plan, and the $2,000 annual maximum is modest, so the maximum, not the 50% coinsurance, is the real limit on an implant or full-arch case. It rewards a buyer who wants to keep their current dentist, needs adult braces, and can either time major work past the 12-month wait or stage a big case across two calendar years to draw on two $2,000 maximums.

Compared with the rest of the shelf: against Humana Extend 5000, Delta leads on network size and is one of the few to cover adult ortho, but Humana carries a higher $5,000 maximum, reaches major at a 6-month wait, and bundles vision (Delta is dental-only). Against Guardian Premier 2.0, Delta covers adult ortho where Guardian is child-only, but Guardian pays 85% on basic from day one with no wait and carries a $3,000 maximum.
