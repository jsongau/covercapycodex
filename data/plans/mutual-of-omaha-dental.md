## Positioning and notes
Mutual of Omaha sells two individual dental insurance policies (Mutual Dental Protection and Mutual Dental Preferred, forms DNT5 and DNT2), both underwritten by Mutual of Omaha Insurance Company and administered at least in part by TruAssure. The CoverCapy featured plan is Mutual Dental Preferred at the $5,000 annual maximum. Its strengths: a high $5,000 maximum option, no waiting period on preventive, basic or major work, community-rated guaranteed-issue pricing, a very broad DenteMax Plus network (400,000+ locations), and a separate $3,000 lifetime implant maximum. The trade-off is that major work, while available day one, is paid at a reduced first-year benefit that ramps up, so the exact early-year reimbursement should be checked on the quote.

Who it is for: someone who wants a high annual ceiling with no waiting period and stable community-rated pricing, including seniors on Medicare and the self-employed.
