## Positioning and notes
Ameritas PrimeStar Complete is the no-waiting-period play. Every category, including major work and implants, is available from day one, and coverage can start as soon as the next business day. Its defining edge over the rest of the shelf is day-one implant access that even covers the bone graft when an extraction narrative establishes the implant as a necessity, making it the strongest day-one implant schedule we track. The trade-off is the year-one major rate of only 20%, which steps up to 50% in year two, so it rewards people who can stage larger restorative cases into the second year, when the annual maximum has also grown from $2,000 to $3,500. Pricing is age-neutral, a real advantage for applicants 65 and older. It bundles a hearing benefit (not in NH) that competing individual dental plans do not offer, and vision is an optional add-on. It does not cover orthodontics or whitening. Compared with UHC Primary Dental it costs more but actually covers major work and implants; compared with Aetna Dental Direct it has no waiting periods where Aetna makes you wait 6 and 12 months (waivable). ## Discrepancies resolved (against official brochures, 2026-06-26)
Verified against Ameritas brochures GR 8228 MAC 2-26 (base-state plan comparison, eff. 4/1/2026), GR 7708 3-26 (Product Guide) and GR 7500 WA 2-26 (WA Advantage Plus), fetched from ameritas.com OCM doc 647311 and doc 540705.

- Annual maximum: CONFIRMED. $2,000 day one rising to $3,500 after year one (Basic and Major combined, preventive excluded). No change.
- Premium: RESOLVED. Brochure GR 7708 3-26 states an average policyholder cost of $55.79/month for Care Complete. Updated the estimate from ~$60 to ~$56 (brochure $55.79). Pages and compare data that say $60 should be reconciled down.
- Network name: RESOLVED. The brochure name is the Ameritas Classic (PPO) network / Ameritas Dental Network. "PrimeStar Network" in plans-data is wrong and should be corrected to Classic (PPO).
- Activation: RESOLVED. Brochures say 'Coverage can begin as soon as tomorrow' / 'comprehensive next-day coverage'. Next-day is correct; same-day refers only to electronic policy/ID card access. Compare-page copy saying 'active within 1 to 3 business days' overstates the lag and should be corrected to next-day.
- Out-of-network percentages: CORRECTED. Prior site copy showed preventive 90% OON and basic 70/80% OON. The GR 8228 MAC table shows preventive 80% OON, basic 60% to 70% OON, major 10% to 30% OON. Pages must be reconciled.
- Implant maximum: CONFIRMED. $1,000 day one, $1,500 year 2+ per person per benefit year, deducted from the annual dental maximum (Type 4).
- Deductible family cap: CLARIFIED. The 'max 3 deductibles per family' note is on the WA Advantage Plus brochure only, not the base-state Care Complete table. Do not assert it as a Care Complete term outside WA.
