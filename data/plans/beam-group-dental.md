## Positioning and notes
Beam is the carrier on our shelf that most matches CoverCapy's own brand: modern, digital, fast. A group can be live in about five business days through Beam's self-serve Lighthouse portal, the Ultra tier carries up to a $5,000 annual maximum, there are no waiting periods by default, composites aren't downgraded to amalgam pricing, and there's no missing-tooth clause — three quiet but real upgrades that employees feel at the chair. Adult and child orthodontia are available on the Select and Ultra tiers.

Who it is for on CoverCapy: the founder of a young, design-conscious company (agencies, startups, boutiques) who wants a benefit that looks and works like the rest of their modern stack, set up in days rather than weeks. It pairs naturally with our luxury, get-covered-fast positioning.

Cost-sharing works the standard group way: employer picks the design and funds a share, employee pays the rest via payroll, dependents move the employee to a higher tier, and the chair-side "copay" is the coinsurance plus deductible. Beam's exact contribution/participation rules are quote-only.

Dentist value angle: Beam patients arrive with no waiting periods, no downgrade games on fillings, and (on Ultra) a $5,000 ceiling — clean, modern claims an accredited office will appreciate.
