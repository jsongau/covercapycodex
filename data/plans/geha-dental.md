## Positioning and notes
GEHA Connection Dental Federal is the other heavyweight federal-employee (FEDVIP) dental plan and a strong one: the High Option has an unlimited annual maximum, pays basic at 80% (member 20%), covers adult-and-child orthodontia at 30% up to $3,500 lifetime with no waiting period, and runs on the 450,000-location Connection Dental network. It even includes a third adult cleaning. Like BCBS FEP Dental, it serves federal employees and retirees who enroll individually during Open Season, so it lives on the federal/individual content track, not the small-business employer hub, but it's a strong SEO magnet for "GEHA dental."

A practical nuance worth surfacing to members: GEHA caps implants at $2,500 per person per year (a real limit on multi-implant cases) and will not take over orthodontia already started with another carrier (except TRICARE).

Dentist value angle: unlimited annual max on High and a large network mean federal-employee patients can pursue extensive major work with strong reimbursement, though implant-heavy cases hit the $2,500/year sub-cap.
