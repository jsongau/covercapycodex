## Positioning and notes
BCBS FEP Dental is the Blue Cross dental plan for the federal workforce, and it is genuinely strong: the High Option has no in-network annual maximum, no waiting periods on anything, adult-and-child orthodontia at 50% up to $3,500 lifetime, and a half-million-dentist national network. It exists for a different audience than the employer hub (federal employees and retirees, who enroll individually through BENEFEDS during Open Season), but it is a magnet for "Blue Cross dental" and "FEP BlueDental" searches, so it earns its own page for SEO and for the federal-employee segment.

Use it on the federal/Blue-brand content track, not the small-business employer hub. For a small business owner, point them to commercial Blue group dental (bcbs-group-dental.md) or Anthem instead.

Dentist value angle: 500,000+ dentists and no annual max on High means federal-employee patients can pursue major work and implants with strong, uncapped in-network reimbursement.
