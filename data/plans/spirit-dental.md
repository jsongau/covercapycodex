## Positioning and notes
Spirit is the high-ceiling, no-waiting individual play, and the only individual plan on our shelf that advertises up to a $5,000 annual maximum (the Pinnacle PPO 5000). Its signature is the $100 lifetime deductible (pay once, never again) plus genuine day-one coverage for preventive, basic, and major, with select plans covering implants and child braces immediately. The honest mechanic to communicate: both the annual maximum and the coinsurance ramp upward over the first one to three years, so a member who stays gets richer benefits over time, the plan rewards loyalty rather than front-loading it.

Who it is for: an individual or family who wants a high ceiling and no waiting, is willing to stay enrolled for the ramp to mature, and likes the one-time deductible. It pairs well with our "see a dentist tomorrow" promise because activation is immediate.

For the $5,000 guardrail: Spirit Pinnacle can carry a "$5,000 maximum" line in the INDIVIDUAL context, but it must be labeled as a matured/ramping cap, and it does not change the employer/group guardrail (group $5,000 remains Humana/Beam/MetLife only).

Dentist value angle: Ameritas Network (149,000+ dentists), immediate activation, and day-one major coverage mean patients can start treatment right away; the year-over-year ramp encourages retention and repeat visits.
