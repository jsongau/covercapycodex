## Positioning and notes
Renaissance is the Delta-family carrier that sells under a non-Delta brand, built for flexible group designs. Its most distinctive assets are the Reward Plan (no waiting periods plus an annual maximum that doubles from year one to year three), the Rollover Maximum rider (bank up to $750 of unused max), and an unusually rich rider menu: Major Services Guarantee (free redo of a failed crown/implant/etc.), Vanishing Deductible, Early Ortho Max Extender, and Orthodontic Reset. For an owner who wants a benefit that rewards loyalty and protects against redo costs, Renaissance has the most interesting feature set on our shelf after Guardian and Ameritas.

Honest caveats: maxes top at $2,500 in published designs (no $5,000), ortho is child-only, and premiums plus several employer-mechanics are quote-only. Importantly, correct the common error that Renaissance is an Ameritas product, it is a Delta Dental affiliate with its own composite network (about 450,000 access points).

Dentist value angle: guaranteed in-network fee acceptance (no balance billing), fast claims (96%+ in 10 days), and loyalty features that drive repeat preventive visits to the chosen office.
