## Headline verdict
For a business owner who wants the strongest dental benefit their employees can actually use — high annual maximum, orthodontia including kids, and no waiting — the honest top three are **Humana group, Beam (Ultra), and MetLife**. These are also the only three carriers that can honestly carry a "$5,000 maximum" banner. Everyone else either caps lower or attaches "$5,000" to an individual product that drops ortho/implants.

## Overall ranking (max coverage + ortho incl. kids + fast usability)
1. **Humana** — only plan with a verified ceiling above $5,000 (selectable $1,000 to Unlimited), child+adult ortho at just 2+ enrolled with a separate $1,000-$2,500 lifetime pool, AND no waiting periods on basic/major/ortho at 5+ enrolled (confirmed on the primary TX SPD). Unique 30% Extended Annual Maximum keeps paying after the ceiling. Cleanest "high ceiling + braces + start now" story.
2. **Beam (Ultra)** — verified $5,000 Ultra max, no waiting periods by default, adult+child ortho on Select/Ultra, plus no missing-tooth clause and no composite downgrade (real chair-side wins nobody else lists). ~5-day digital setup. Most on-brand for CoverCapy.
3. **MetLife** — documented $5,000 "Platinum" PDP Plus design with adult+child ortho to $2,000 lifetime, blue-chip network. But $5,000 is one PEO design (not universal) and the standard design carries waits that are only "commonly" waived — usability is more conditional.
4. **Aetna** — no waiting periods (fair late-entrant rule); richer "Gold" design pays a generous 80% on major AND implants with adult+child ortho to $2,000. Ceiling caps at $2,500 commercial (Unlimited only on federal FEDVIP).
5. **Ameritas** — a $5,000 marketplace tier exists and adult+child ortho is standard (to $2,500), usually no waits — but the $5,000 tier's exact design is unconfirmed (image-only source), so it can't carry the banner yet.
6. **Delta** — no waits at any size, largest US network, clear aligners free, adult+child ortho available; but group ortho-bearing designs cap ~$2,000-$3,000 and the $5,000 plan is individual-only (excludes ortho/implants).
7. **Guardian** — no waits at 5+, adult+child ortho ($1,500), standout Maximum Rollover; no published tier above ~$2,000.
8. **Principal** — no waits, cleanest takeover (deductibles waived, max refreshed, ortho credit), Maximum Accumulation rollover; but maxes top at $2,000 and filed ortho is child-only.
9. **UnitedHealthcare** — ortho gated hard (10+ emp/8+ enrolled), implants excluded from base (FlexAppeal buy-up), base max ~$1,500; MaxMultiplier + medical bundling are the draws.
10. **Cigna** — clean ACA rules, recognizable network; but maxes cap at $2,000, base tiers drop ortho AND implants, and ortho/implant waits are NOT waivable by takeover.
11. **Mutual of Omaha** — last by necessity: the group product's max, deductible, coinsurance, ortho and implants are all quote-only/UNVERIFIED (file is marked partial). Do not rank higher until a real group schedule is obtained.

## Segment winners
- Maximum coverage / highest ceiling: **Humana** (to Unlimited; 30% extended max). Runner-up: Beam Ultra ($5,000).
- Smallest businesses (2-3 employees): **Principal** (writes employer-paid down to 2; 25 in MA). Also 2+: Humana, Beam, Delta, Guardian.
- Fastest to set up: **Beam** (~5 days via Lighthouse). Runner-up: Guardian (~2 weeks).
- Best for families needing braces: **Humana** (child+adult ortho at 2+, no ortho deductible, separate lifetime pool, no wait at 5+, Invisalign included).
- Best rollover/carryover value: **Guardian** (Maximum Rollover; generous account limit). Close: Ameritas Dental Rewards, Principal Maximum Accumulation (both forfeit all accumulation if no claim filed that year).
- Best brand/network recognition: **Delta** (largest US network: Premier 154,000+ dentists / 330,000+ locations).
- Most modern/digital: **Beam** (digital-first, AI underwriting, Lighthouse portal).

## The "$5,000 maximum" rule (compliance guardrail)
GRANT the $5,000 banner only to: **Humana** (to Unlimited; $5,000 a valid point), **Beam** (Ultra tier), **MetLife** (one documented PEO Platinum design — qualify it). Ameritas's $5,000 tier exists but keep it OFF the banner until a broker confirms the grid.
DENY for: Delta group ($5,000 is the individual Choice 5000, which EXCLUDES ortho and implants — web-confirmed), Guardian ($2K), UHC (Unlimited=FEDVIP; the "$5,000" is a bundling credit, not a max), Aetna ($2.5K; Unlimited=FEDVIP), Cigna ($2K), Principal ($2K), Mutual of Omaha (individual-only).

## Recommended hero + runner-up for the hub
