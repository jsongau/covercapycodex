Insurance

How PPO coverage works here

A lot of people searching for KYT are really trying to figure out what their PPO plan pays before they sit in the chair. The office is built around that. They call your plan, confirm what is left on your benefits, and write the numbers down before treatment is scheduled.

It helps to know a few of your own details first: whether your plan is active, what is left on your annual maximum, and whether bigger work like a crown or implant has a waiting period. The point is fewer surprises on the day of the appointment.

If you want to sort out coverage before you go, compare PPO dental plans or run a quick treatment cost estimate first, then come back and book.

Note: KYT works with PPO plans and does not contract with HMO or Medi-Cal. Always confirm your specific plan with the office.

Good questions to ask before treatment

  • Is my PPO plan active right now?
  • What is left on my annual maximum this year?
  • Do crowns, implants, or deep cleanings have a waiting period?
  • Will my plan downgrade a crown or filling?
  • Can you send a pre-treatment estimate before major work?
  • What will I owe after the insurance estimate?
