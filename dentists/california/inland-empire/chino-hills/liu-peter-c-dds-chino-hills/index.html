The Practice

Serving Chino Hills since 1989

Liu Dental Care was established by Dr. Peter C. Liu DDS, who built his practice on Los Serranos Country Club Drive before the neighborhood was fully developed. Over 35 years, the same families have returned — and now their children and grandchildren do too.

Today, Dr. Aaron T. Liu — Dr. Peter's son — practices alongside his father and Dr. Marie Nguyen, making Liu Dental Care one of the few genuinely multi-generational family dental practices in the Inland Empire. The practice runs on a straightforward philosophy: see the same families for decades, earn trust through honest dentistry, and never make a patient feel rushed.
