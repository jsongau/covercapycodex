PPO insurance

How PPO coverage works here

Liu Dental Care participates in the Ameritas PPO network. If you have Ameritas, your in-network benefits apply — call (909) 606-6888 to confirm your specific plan details before scheduling.

If you have a different PPO plan, most PPO plans include out-of-network benefits. The office can verify your coverage and give you a pre-treatment cost estimate before anything is scheduled. Call with your member ID ready.

Important: Network participation changes. Always confirm with the office directly — do not rely solely on this listing for insurance decisions.

Questions about Liu Dental Care

Where is Liu Dental Care?

15942 Los Serranos Country Club Drive, Suite F, Chino Hills, CA 91709 — on the road adjacent to the Los Serranos Country Club golf course, in the south end of Chino Hills.

Does Liu Dental Care accept PPO insurance?

Yes. Liu Dental Care participates in the Ameritas PPO network. If you have a different PPO plan, call (909) 606-6888 — most PPO plans include out-of-network benefits and the office can verify yours before you book.

Is there a Saturday appointment?

Yes. Saturday hours are 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM. The office is also open Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, and Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Closed Wednesday and Sunday. Call ahead to confirm availability.

What languages do they speak?

English, Mandarin, Spanish, Tagalog, Vietnamese, and Farsi. Call (909) 606-6888 before your appointment to confirm which staff are available on your preferred day.

Who are the dentists?

Dr. Peter C. Liu DDS (founder), Dr. Marie Nguyen DDS, and Dr. Aaron T. Liu DDS. Dr. Peter and his son Dr. Aaron practice together — two generations of the Liu family serving the same neighborhood since 1989.

How do I make an appointment?

Call (909) 606-6888 or text (909) 344-5415. The office is open Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri 9am–6pm and Saturday 8am–1pm.
