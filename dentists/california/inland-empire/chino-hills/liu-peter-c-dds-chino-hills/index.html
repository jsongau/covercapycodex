The short version

A Chino Hills PPO dentist that's been at the same corner since 1989

Liu Dental Care is at 15942 Los Serranos Country Club Drive, Suite F — on the road running alongside the Los Serranos Country Club golf course. If you know the Los Serranos neighborhood, the office is right there on the corner.

Most people who look up Liu Dental Care want the same few answers: does it take my PPO plan, can I get a Saturday appointment, and do they speak my language. This page puts all of that in one place.

Two generations of the Liu family practice here: Dr. Peter C. Liu DDS, who founded the practice, and his son Dr. Aaron T. Liu DDS, who joined alongside Dr. Marie Nguyen DDS. Patients who came in as children in the 1990s are now bringing their own kids.

Questions

Questions about Liu Dental Care

Where is Liu Dental Care located?

15942 Los Serranos Country Club Drive, Suite F, Chino Hills, CA 91709 — on the road adjacent to the Los Serranos Country Club golf course. From the 71 Freeway, exit Chino Hills Pkwy south and turn east on Los Serranos Country Club Dr.

Does Liu Dental Care accept PPO insurance?

Yes. Liu Dental Care participates in the Ameritas PPO network (confirmed June 2026). The CoverCapy directory also lists Aetna, Cigna, Delta Dental, and MetLife — call (909) 606-6888 with your member ID to verify your specific plan before scheduling.

Is there a Saturday appointment available?

Yes. Saturday hours are 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM. The office is also open Mon, Tue, Thu, and Fri 9am–6pm. Closed Wednesday and Sunday. Call ahead — (909) 606-6888 — to confirm Saturday availability.

What languages are spoken at Liu Dental Care?

English, Mandarin Chinese, Spanish, Tagalog, Vietnamese, and Farsi. Call (909) 606-6888 before your appointment to confirm which staff members are available on your preferred day.

Who are the dentists at Liu Dental Care?

Dr. Peter C. Liu DDS (founder, since 1989), Dr. Marie Nguyen DDS, and Dr. Aaron T. Liu DDS — Dr. Peter's son. Two generations of the Liu family practice together, making this one of the few genuinely multi-generational dental practices in the Inland Empire.

How far is Liu Dental Care from Diamond Bar?

Approximately 10 minutes via Grand Avenue west into Chino Hills, then to Los Serranos Country Club Drive. No freeway needed from most of Diamond Bar.

Is Liu Dental Care near Cal Poly Pomona or Ontario Airport?

Cal Poly Pomona is approximately 8 miles north via the 71 Freeway. Ontario International Airport is approximately 14 miles east via the 60 or 71. Practical for Inland Empire patients who work or travel through the eastern IE.

How do I make an appointment?

Call (909) 606-6888 or text (909) 344-5415. Office hours: Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri 9am–6pm · Saturday 8am–1pm. Closed Wednesday and Sunday.
