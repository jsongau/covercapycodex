Practice History

Serving Chino Hills families since 1989

Liu Dental Care was established in 1989 — when Chino Hills itself was still a young and rapidly growing community in southern San Bernardino County. The practice has grown alongside the city, serving multiple generations of families in the Los Serranos neighborhood, throughout Chino Hills, and across the broader Inland Empire region including Chino, Diamond Bar, Walnut, and Phillips Ranch.

The practice is led today by Dr. Peter C. Liu DDS, who founded it, along with Dr. Marie Nguyen DDS and Dr. Aaron T. Liu DDS. The multi-generational nature of the practice reflects a continuity of care model that long-term patients in Chino Hills have valued for over three decades.

Located at 15942 Los Serranos Country Club Drive, Suite F — on the same road as the historic Los Serranos Country Club — the practice serves patients who live and work in the surrounding area, including residents of the Chino Hills State Park corridor, families near Chino Hills High School, and shoppers who frequent The Shoppes at Chino Hills.
