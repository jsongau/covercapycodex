My recommendation

Concierge Hairline (1) as the everyday base — it's the most on-brand, fastest, and ages best. Pair it with Goldspark Gleam (5) applied only to Capy Rewards, so the one gamified tab gets the variable-reward delight where it actually fits the product story, while the rest of the bar stays editorial and calm.

If you want the whole bar to feel more alive, Lustre (4) is the highest-"wow" but the heaviest; Rising Tide (2) and Status Jewel (3) are strong middle-ground options. Tell me which one (or combination) and I'll wire it into the real nav in nav-prototype-v3.html.
