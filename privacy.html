Privacy Policy

Last updated May 30, 2026

CoverCapy respects visitor privacy. This policy explains how CoverCapy may collect, use, and protect information when people use our website, comparison tools, dentist discovery pages, treatment cost resources, contact forms, and related experiences.

Information we may collect

  • Information users submit, such as name, email address, phone number, ZIP code, insurance interests, dentist requests, form responses, or messages.
  • Usage information, such as pages viewed, links clicked, approximate location derived from IP address, device type, browser type, referring page, and site performance data.
  • Cookie and advertising identifiers used for analytics, security, personalization, measurement, and advertising.

How we may use information

  • To respond to requests and provide PPO dental guidance, dentist discovery, plan comparison, treatment estimate context, and customer support.
  • To improve website content, usability, security, performance, and user experience.
  • To measure marketing performance and understand how visitors interact with CoverCapy.
  • To comply with legal, security, fraud prevention, and operational requirements.

Google AdSense, cookies, and advertising partners

CoverCapy may use Google AdSense and other advertising or measurement services. Third party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables Google and its partners to serve ads based on visits to CoverCapy and other sites on the internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Users may learn more about Google advertising technologies at Google's advertising policy page.
  • Users may also visit AboutAds.info to learn about choices for participating companies.

Analytics and site measurement

CoverCapy may use analytics tools, including Google Analytics or similar services, to understand traffic, improve content, and diagnose technical issues. These tools may use cookies, device identifiers, and aggregated usage data.

Sharing of information

CoverCapy does not sell personal information to advertisers. Information may be shared with service providers, analytics vendors, advertising technology providers, dental offices or partners when a user requests a connection, and other parties when required for security, legal compliance, or site operations.

Dental and insurance information

Users should avoid submitting sensitive medical information unless a secure workflow specifically asks for it. CoverCapy does not provide dental, medical, legal, financial, or insurance advice through this website.

Privacy choices

Users may limit cookies through browser settings. Some website features may not work correctly without cookies. Users may contact CoverCapy to request access, correction, deletion, or review of information they have submitted, subject to applicable law and verification.

Contact

Privacy questions may be sent through the Contact page.
