Platinum Elite
The top of the CoverCapy hierarchy

The short version

Platinum Elite is the highest membership tier in CoverCapy's dental directory. It is $1,499 per month, limited at the county level so only a small number of practices hold it in any market, and it requires a practice to already be Capy Accredited. It carries the widest 18-mile search reach, top-of-directory placement, and premium positioning to benefit-ready PPO patients. It is granted by review, not by payment alone.

What It Is

The top of the CoverCapy hierarchy

CoverCapy ranks practices in four levels: an unclaimed listing, a claimed profile, a Capy Accredited office, and Platinum Elite. Platinum Elite sits at the very top.

It is the highest and most selective level of Capy Accreditation, reserved for offices with advanced technology, a proven patient experience, and the infrastructure to run premium CoverCapy programs well. It is reviewed and limited. It is not a license, and it is not a guarantee of any outcome.

For the right practice, it is a members-club position: a small, curated group at the top of the market, presented with the prestige that placement deserves.

Scarce By Design

County-level exclusivity

A premium position only holds value when it stays rare. Platinum Elite is limited by geography and local market density, so only a small number of practices can hold it in a given county.

The strongest dental directories and the best marketing partners have learned the same lesson: when one practice per area gets the premium slot, that slot is worth something. Most large PPO directories list everyone by ZIP and treat every office the same. Platinum Elite does the opposite.

When a county already has its Platinum Elite practices, the market is full. New applicants join a review queue and are considered as places open. You are not buying a slot, you are being reviewed for one of a limited number of them.

Selection considers technology readiness, patient trust, service capability, market participation, and existing Platinum presence in your area.

Eligibility

You must be Capy Accredited first

Platinum Elite is the highest level of Capy Accreditation, so it sits on top of the credential, not beside it.

Step one is always Capy Accreditation. A practice must already be Capy Accredited before it can progress to Platinum Elite review.

From there, the Platinum review looks for the capabilities and infrastructure to deliver premium programs well:

  • iTero scanning
  • Advanced diagnostics, CBCT strongly preferred
  • Same day crown technology preferred
  • In office crown milling preferred
  • Invisalign or clear aligner capability
  • Cosmetic dentistry and veneers
  • Implant and full mouth rehabilitation
  • TMJ and therapeutic Botox
  • Digital smile planning
  • In house whitening and retainer workflows
  • Chair time and staffing for promotions
  • Verified patient testimonials may be requested
  • Ability to participate in structured CoverCapy programs

Platinum Elite is reviewed and limited. It is not available by payment alone. Offices are assessed for capability, patient experience, and the infrastructure to deliver premium programs well, and a place must be open in your county.

Is Platinum Elite a dental license or certification?+
No. Platinum Elite is a private CoverCapy credential and directory tier. It is not a government certification or a dental board license and does not replace either. It does not guarantee a clinical outcome. It signals a reviewed, technology forward, premium practice inside CoverCapy.
