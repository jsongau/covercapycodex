Field Guide · No. 04
The PPO Field Guide p. 14

What is PPO dental insurance?

APreferred Provider Organization plan is, at its core, a negotiated discount: your insurance carrier and a network of dentists agree on a fee schedule, and you pay the contracted rate instead of the open-market price. No assigned dentist. No referrals. No surprise gatekeeping.

❝ When a dentist is in-network with a PPO insurance plan, they cannot bill above the contracted rate. The insurance then covers what remains, often 100% of preventive care and a sliding scale for the rest.
